Income Discrepancy Report

Identifies households where there is a difference of $2,400 or more in the wage, unemployment and SSA benefit information reported in EIV and wage, unemployment and SSA benefit information reported in TRACS for the period of income used for discrepancy analysis.

The report serves as a tool to alert O/As that there may be a discrepancy in the income reported by the tenant during the period of income used for the discrepancy analysis.
