# 8-10 Allowable Use of Security Deposits

## Table-of-contents context

- Chapter 8. Termination
  - Section: Termination Of Tenancy By Lessees
    - 8-10 Allowable Use of Security Deposits

## Source text

If a tenant fails to pay the required rent or if there are tenant damages to the unit, an owner may use the tenant’s security deposit to pay the outstanding rent and/or damages.

Any remaining funds must be paid to the tenant. An owner must follow the requirements and guidelines for security deposits and other charges outlined in paragraph 6-18 regarding the refunding and use of the security deposit.
