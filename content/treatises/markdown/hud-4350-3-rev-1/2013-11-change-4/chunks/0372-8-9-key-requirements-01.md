# 8-9 Key Requirements

  - Section: Termination Of Tenancy By Lessees

## Source text

In order to terminate tenancy, the tenant must provide the owner with a written 30-day notice to vacate the unit, as required by the HUD lease.

NOTE: The regulations for RHS Section 515/8 properties permit either the tenant or the owner to terminate the lease with a 30-day written notice. This provision may be included in a one-year lease. The provision must be included in any multi-year lease.
