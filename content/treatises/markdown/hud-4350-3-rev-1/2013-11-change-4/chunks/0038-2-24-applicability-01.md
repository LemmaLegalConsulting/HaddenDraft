## Source text

This section covers the nondiscrimination and accessibility requirements applicable to the occupancy of existing housing for which the owner receives federal financial assistance.

NOTE: For the related accessibility requirements that apply to the development of new properties, refer to the HUD Handbooks and other HUD guidance specific to the program providing assistance to the project, the Section 504 regulations and program regulations.
