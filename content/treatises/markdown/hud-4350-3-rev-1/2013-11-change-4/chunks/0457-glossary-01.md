A family whose head, spouse, or sole member is a person with disabilities (as defined by 24 CFR 5.403). It may include two or more persons with disabilities living together, or one or more persons with disabilities living with one or more live-in aides. [24 CFR 5.403] (See definition of Person with Disabilities as defined for program eligibility purposes.)
