# 5-22 Interim Recertifications

## Table-of-contents context

- Chapter 5. Determining Income And Calculating Rent
  - Section: Verification

## Source text

When processing an interim recertification, the owner must ask the tenant to identify all changes in income, expenses, or family composition since the last recertification.

Owners only need verify those items that have changed. For example, if the head of household was laid off from his or her job and asks the owner to prepare an interim recertification, the owner does not need to reverify the spouse’s employment income unless that has also changed. When the tenant signs the certification she or he certifies that the information on the report is accurate and current. Additional information about the procedures for conducting interim recertifications is discussed in Chapter 7, Section 2.
