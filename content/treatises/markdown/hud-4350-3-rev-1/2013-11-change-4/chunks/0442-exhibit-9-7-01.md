3. Identifying the Actual Income Reported during the Period of Income

Actual income information is used to evaluate the accuracy of an income
projection. It is compared to the projected income value stored on the form HUD- 50059 in TRACS associated with the household. These values are:

Income Code Type of Income B Business F Federal Wage M Military Pay W Nonfederal Wage U Unemployment SS Social Security SI Supplemental Security Income

Note: Other income the household receives, e.g., welfare benefits, most pensions, child support, etc., may be reported in annual income in TRACS but it is not used for the discrepancy analysis in EIV.
