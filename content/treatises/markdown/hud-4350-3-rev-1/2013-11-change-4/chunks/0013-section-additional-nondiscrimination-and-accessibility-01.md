Requirements for Persons with Disabilities explains the requirements and procedures that owners of HUD-subsidized multifamily housing must follow to ensure nondiscrimination and accessibility of their properties to persons with disabilities as required by Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973 and the Fair Housing Act.
