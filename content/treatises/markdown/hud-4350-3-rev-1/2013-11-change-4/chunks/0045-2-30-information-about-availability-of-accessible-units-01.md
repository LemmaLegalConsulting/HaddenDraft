# 2-30 Information about Availability of Accessible Units

## Table-of-contents context

- Chapter 2. Civil Rights And Nondiscrimination Requirements
  - Section: Additional Nondiscrimination And Accessibility Requirements For
    - 2-30 Information about Availability of Accessible Units

## Source text

A. Owners must have policies and practices to ensure that information about the availability of accessible units reaches eligible persons with disabilities. (See Chapter 4, Section 2, for information about marketing.)
B. HUD also encourages owners to maintain contact with sources/agencies in the community who provide services to persons with disabilities so that, when accessible units become available, persons in need of these units may have the opportunity to live in them.
