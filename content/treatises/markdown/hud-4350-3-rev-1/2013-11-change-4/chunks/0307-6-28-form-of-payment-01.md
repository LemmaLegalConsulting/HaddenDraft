# 6-28 Form of Payment

## Table-of-contents context

- Chapter 6. Lease Requirements And Leasing Activities
  - Section: The Leasing Process
    - 6-28 Form of Payment

## Source text

A. An owner may require any tenant to pay the security deposit or the last month's rent in a guaranteed form (e.g., money order, cashier's check, bank check).
B. In all other instances, an owner must accept a tenant's personal check.
C. If the tenant bounces a rent check, thereafter the owner may refuse to accept the tenant's personal check. The owner may require the tenant to pay rent in a guaranteed form as identified above.

REMINDER: Owners must be consistent in their treatment of all tenants.
