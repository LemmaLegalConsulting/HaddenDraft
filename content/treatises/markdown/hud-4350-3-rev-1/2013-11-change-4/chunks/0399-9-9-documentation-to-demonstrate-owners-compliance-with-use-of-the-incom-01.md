# 9-9 Documentation to Demonstrate Owners Compliance with Use of the Income

## Source text

Report

The following documentation is required to be in the tenant file to demonstrate the owner’s compliance with mandated use of EIV as the third party source to verify tenant employment and income information.

A. No Dispute of EIV Information: EIV Income Report, current acceptable tenant- provided documentation and, if necessary (as determined by the owner), third party verification from the source.

B. Disputed EIV Information: EIV Income Report and third party verification from the source for disputed information.

C. Tenant-reported Income Not Verified through the EIV System: EIV Income Report, current acceptable tenant-provided documents or third party verification from the source.

See Paragraph 9-11 and Exhibit 9-5, Use of EIV Reports, for documentation requirements for all EIV reports.
