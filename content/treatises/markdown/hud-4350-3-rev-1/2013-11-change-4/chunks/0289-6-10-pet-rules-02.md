Leases and Lease Attachments

(2) The pet rule violation is sufficient to begin procedures to terminate the pet owner’s tenancy under the terms of the lease and applicable regulations.
b. The property owner may initiate procedures at any time in accordance with the provision of applicable state or local laws. If the state or local provisions conflict with the 10 days that the pet owner is given to correct the violation (see subparagraph E.2.c above), then the timeframe that is most beneficial to the pet owner must be followed.
