This contact information will assist the owner in providing the delivery of services or special care to the tenant and assist in any tenancy issues arising during the term of tenancy of the tenant.
4. Retention and confidentiality of contact information.
a. If the applicant does not become a tenant, the owner will retain the form HUD-92006 with the application for three years. (See Paragraph 4-22.B)
b. If the applicant becomes a tenant, the owner will retain the form HUD-92006 with the application for the term of tenancy plus three
years. (See Paragraph 4-22.C)
c. Owners must keep the contact information confidential. Owners are allowed to release the information for the stated statutory purpose only: To assist the owners in providing services or special care for such tenants, and in resolving issues that may arise during the tenancy of such tenants.*
