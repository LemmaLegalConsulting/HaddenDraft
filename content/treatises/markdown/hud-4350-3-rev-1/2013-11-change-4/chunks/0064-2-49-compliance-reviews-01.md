## Source text

Compliance reviews are conducted by FHEO staff in accordance with Departmental
procedures. The procedures for FHEO reviews of Title VI requirements are discussed in HUD FHEO Handbook 8040.1, Compliance and Enforcement Procedures for Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964. The procedures related to compliance with the Fair Housing Act are covered in HUD FHEO Handbook 8024.1, Title VIII Complaint, Intake, Investigation, and Conciliation Handbook.
