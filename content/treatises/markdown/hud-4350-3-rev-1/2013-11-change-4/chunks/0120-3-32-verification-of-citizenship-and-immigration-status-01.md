# 3-32 Verification of Citizenship and Immigration Status

## Table-of-contents context

- Chapter 3. Eligibility For Assistance And Occupancy
  - Section: Verification Of Eligibility Factors
    - 3-32 Verification of Citizenship and Immigration Status

## Source text

A. In properties subject to the restriction on assistance to noncitizens (see paragraph 3-12 F), owners may require that applicants provide verification of citizenship and must require that noncitizens provide verification of immigration
status. The verification process for immigration status is dependent upon receiving information from the DHS. Because the process of verification can involve a number of steps and may result in "partial" eligibility, verification of immigration status has been covered in Section 1 of this chapter.
B. Access to Services for Persons with LEP. Housing owners must take reasonable steps to ensure meaningful access to the information and services they provide for persons with LEP. This may include interpreter services and/or written materials translated into other languages. See HUD Guidance referenced in Paragraph 2-9.C for further details.
