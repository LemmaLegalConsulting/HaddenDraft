## Source text

The Fair Housing Act prohibits discrimination in housing on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, disability, familial status, or national origin. Owners are responsible for ensuring that the policies and practices used in properties covered by this handbook do not incorporate prohibited practices. This section provides an overview of these
requirements. Owners are fully responsible for understanding and complying with the requirements applicable to their properties.
