# 4. Sale/Purchase of the Home

## Source text

A park operator may not evict a mh park owner based on a sale of the manufactured
home. R.C. 4781.40(H)(2) (former R.C. 3733.11(H)(2)). E.g., Freyermuth v. Navarre
Vill. Homes Austin Square, Inc. , No. 1999 -CA-00187, 2000 WL 94048 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., Jan. 24, 2000); Cmty. Gardens Park & Sales, Inc. v. Roe, No. M8109-CVG- 027176 (Ohio Mun., Franklin Cty., Nov. 6, 1981); Mentor Trailer Park, Inc. v. Clark,
No. 79-CVG-492 (Ohio Mun., Mentor, June 7, 1979).

A park operator may not evict a purchaser of a manufactured home, located in the mh park, based on the seller’s refusal to hire the park operator as the agent for the sale.

R.C. 4781.40(F),(K) (former R.C. 3733.11(F),(K)). E.g., Jameson v. Sommer’s Mobile Home Sales, Inc., No. 83769 (Ohio C.P., Lorain Cty., May 13, 1980).

A park operator may not unreasonably refuse to enter into a rental agreement with a purchaser of a manufactured home, located within the mh park. R.C. 4781.40(H)(3) (former R.C. 3733.11(H)(3)). E.g., Kantner v. Gibson, No. 2-07-17, 2007 WL 414177 (Ohio App., 3rd Dist., Nov. 26, 2007); Czerwonko v. Sahara Mobile Home Park & Sales, Inc., 137 Ohio App.3d 266, 738 N.E.2d 461 (11th Dist. 2000);White v. Superior Mobile Homes, Inc., No. 98-T-0011, 1999 WL 1320188 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Dec.

10, 1999); Santilla v. Sahara Mobile Home Park & Sales , No. 93 -L-028, 1993 WL 407233 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Sept. 30, 1993). For additional court decisions on this issue, see Section XXI.C.4.d (sale of home), below.
