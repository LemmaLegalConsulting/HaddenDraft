# 3. Court Reporter

## Source text

For the trial in an eviction action, the court is required to provide an official court reporter to record the evidence, if a party to the action properly requests those services.

State ex rel. Brady v. Youngstown Mun. Court, No. 88-CA-162, 1990 WL 18139(Ohio App., 7th Dist., Feb. 26, 1990).

The local court rules may permit a party to retain the services of a private reporter who the court may then designate as the official court reporter for the trial.
