# k. Self-Defense

## Source text

In eviction actions for alleged criminal activity, if thetenant’s conduct falls within the privilege of self-defense, the tenant’s conduct does not constitute unlawful activity for the eviction claim. Millennia Hous. Mgmt. Ltd. v. Mitchell, No. 2013-CVG-008410 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, Hous. Div., Nov. 19, 2013).
