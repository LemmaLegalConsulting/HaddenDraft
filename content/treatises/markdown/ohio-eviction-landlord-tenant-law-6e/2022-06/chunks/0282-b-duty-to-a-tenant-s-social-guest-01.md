# b. Duty to a Tenant’s Social Guest

## Source text

“[A] landlord owes to a tenant’s guest the same duty that the landlord owes a tenant.”264 Mann v. Northgate Investors, L.L.C., 138 Ohio St.3d 175, 5 N.E.3d 594, ¶ 33 (2014). Accord Shump v. First Cont’l-Robinwood Assocs., 71 Ohio St.3d 414, 644 N.E.2d 291 (1994).

Therefore, all of the duties that a landlord owes a tenant under R.C. 5321.04(A) extend to a tenant’s guest, where applicable.Mann v. Northgate Investors, L.L.C., 138 Ohio St.3d 175, 5 N.E.3d 594 (2014); Shump v. First Cont’l-Robinwood Assocs., 71 Ohio St.3d 414, 644 N.E.2d 291 (1994). It is irrelevant whether the duty concerns the common areas or the tenant’s dwelling unit.Mann v. Northgate Investors, L.L.C., 138 Ohio St.3d 175, 5 N.E.3d 594, ¶ 23 (2014).

The Mann decision, in holding that the landlord’s R.C. 5321.04(A)(3) common area duty extends to a tenant’s guest, resolved a conflict among the appellate districts.
