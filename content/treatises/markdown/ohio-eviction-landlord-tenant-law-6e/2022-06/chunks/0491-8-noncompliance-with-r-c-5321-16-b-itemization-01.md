Rule A landlord’s failure to comply with the R.C. 5321.16(B) written itemization requirement does not waive a landlord’s right to bring a monetar y claim (or
counterclaim) for unpaid rent or property damage. Trimble v. Rossi, No. 108683, 2020 WL 4218808 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., July 23, 2020) (property damage);Vlcek v. Brogee,
No. 25499, 2013 WL 5436930 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Sept. 27, 2013) (proper ty damage); Knipp v. Sadler, No. 6-09-04, 2009 WL 2710053 (Ohio App., 3rd Dist., Aug.

31, 2009) (rent and property damage); Adams v. Davenport, No. 2005-CA-108, 2006 WL 2578852 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Sept. 8, 2006) (rent and property damage);Kelley
v. Johnston, No. 01-CA-5, 2001 WL 1479243 (Ohio App., 4th Dist., Nov. 14, 2001) (property damage); Quinn v. Humphrey, No. 92-L-141, 1993 WL 262582 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., June 30, 1993) (property damage); Scott v. Rice , No. 9 -92-48, 1993 WL 46480 (Ohio App., 3rd Dist., Feb. 11, 1993) (property damage); Sherwin v. Cabana Club Apts., 70 Ohio App.2d 11, 433 N.E.2d 932 (8th Dist. 1980) (property damage).
