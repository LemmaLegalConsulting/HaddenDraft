# e. Vacation Date

## Source text

A notice to vacate is not required to state a specific date by which the tenant is to vacate the premises. Andrews Sch. v. Brown, No. 10-109, 1984 WL 7259 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Sept. 21, 1984);Brandy v. Fontroy, No. 77-CVG-9209 (Ohio Mun., Dayton,
Dec. 14, 1977).

A notice to vacate is also not required to reference the three -day period that must elapse before the landlord may file the eviction action. Andrews Sch. v. Brown , No.

10-109, 1984 WL 7259 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Sept. 21, 1984).
