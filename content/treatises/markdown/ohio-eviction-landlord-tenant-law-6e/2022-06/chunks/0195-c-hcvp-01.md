## Source text

In the Housing Choice Voucher program (“HCVP”),under HUD rules, a two-fold time period is used in all cases. First, the notice of termination may be served at or before the commencement of the eviction action. 24 C.F.R. § 982.310(e). Second, it must be served before the termination of the tenancy.24 C.F.R. § 982.310(e) (“tenancy does not terminate before the owner has given this notice”). See also 64 Fed. Reg.

56893, 56904 (Oct. 21, 1999) (“Section 982.310(e) has been revised to specify that the tenancy does not terminate during the term of the lease before the owner has given notice to the tenant that specifies the grounds for termination”) ; 42 U.S.C.

§ 1437f(d)(1)(B)(iv) (“termination of tenancy shall be preceded by the owner’s provision of written notice to the tenant specifying the grounds for such action”).

Under Ohio law, the seco nd part of the HCVP time period arguably requires service of the notice of termination before service of the R.C. 1923.04 notice to vacate.
