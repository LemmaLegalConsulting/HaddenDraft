## Source text

The defense of comparative negligence may be asserted in an R.C. 5321.04 negligence per se claim. Parker v. Vaughn, No. 16939, 1998 WL 639305, at *4 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Aug. 7, 1998); Hubbard v. Crutchfield, No. CA96-06-050, 1997 WL 162397, at *2 (Ohio App., 12th Dist., Apr. 7, 1997); Guthrie v. Jamison , No. 94 - APE10-1560, 1995 WL 238425, at *3 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., Apr. 20, 1995); Brazen
v. Brazzon, No. 50293, 1986 WL 3233, at *4 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Mar. 13, 1986).

See also Burdick v. Nevel , No. 98AP-697, 1999 WL 235435, at *1 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., Apr. 20, 1999); Martin v. Edgewater Props. , No. 92 -CA-005307, 1992 WL 357321, at *3-4 (Ohio App., 9th Dist., Nov. 25, 1992).
