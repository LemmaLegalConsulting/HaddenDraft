## Source text

In an eviction action, the court must determine whether to enter a judgment for the landlord or for the tenant and, if the judgment is for the landlord, when and against whom to issue the writ of restitution (also known as the writ of execution). The writ of restitution is discussed in Section I.O.2, below. Issues concerning the persons against whom an eviction judgment may be enforced are discussed in Section I.O.3, below.
