## Source text

In the administrative hearing decision on whether to terminate a family’s participation in the HCVP, the factual determinations must be based on the evidence presented at the hearing. 24 C.F.R. § 982.555(e)(6).

For example, the hearing officer may not investigate and secure evidence after the hearing, and then rely on that evidence in the hearing decision. Lyons v. Tuscarawas
Metro. Hous. Auth., No. 2007-AP-080051, 2009 WL 2781802 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., June 24, 2008).408 Cf. Jackson v. Greene Metro Hous.
