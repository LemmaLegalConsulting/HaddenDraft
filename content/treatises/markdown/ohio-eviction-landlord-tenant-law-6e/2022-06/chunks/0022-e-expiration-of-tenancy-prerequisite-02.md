23 Sandefur Co. v. Jones , 9 Ohio App.3d 85, 458 N.E.2d 390 (10th Dist. 1982); 17 Bella Vista Apts. v. Herzner , 125 Ohio Misc.2d 1, 796 N.E.2d 593 (Ohio Mun., Clermont Cty., 2003). See also Knoll Group Mgmt. Co. v. Wolfe , Nos. 93 -CA-553,554, 1994 WL 326347 (Ohio App., 4th Dist., June 28,1994) (permitting service of federal notice of termination during pendency of eviction action); New Boston Kiwanis Hous., Etc.
v. Sparks, No. 1957, 1992 WL 79561 (Ohio App., 4th Dist., Apr. 14, 1992) (permitting a second notice of termination to cure the content defect of the first notice of termination, if the second notice is served “timely” before the case comes to trial).
