# 2. Limited Liability Company Without Counsel

## Source text

An R.C. Chapter 1705 limited liability company (“limited liability company”), like a corporation, may not commence or maintain a court action without an attorney. In re ICLNDS Notes Acquisition, LLC, 259 B.R. 289 (Bankr. N.D. Ohio 2001) (applying Ohio law).

Therefore, a limited liability company may not file or maintain an eviction action without an attorney. Ohio State Bar Ass’n v. Ross, 154 Ohio St.3d 328, 114 N.E.3d 179 (2018); Kasapis v. If a limited liability company files or maintains an eviction action without an attorney, it is grounds to dismiss the action. Id.
