# 1. Permissive Counterclaims

## Table-of-contents context

- I. Eviction Action, Procedures
  - I. Counterclaims
    - 1. Permissive Counterclaims

## Source text

In an eviction action, the tenant may file a permissive counterclaim under Civil Rule 13(B). Jemo Assocs. v. Garman , 70 Ohio St.2d 267, 436 N.E.2d 1353 (1982).

See also Swaney v. Syndicate Mgmt., Inc. , No. 71422, 1997 WL 2 09223 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Apr. 24, 1997).
