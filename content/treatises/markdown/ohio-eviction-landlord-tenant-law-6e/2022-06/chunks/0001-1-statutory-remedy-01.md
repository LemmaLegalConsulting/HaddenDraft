# 1. Statutory Remedy

## Source text

R.C. Chapter 1923 establishes Ohio’s forcible entry and detainer (“FED”) action, which is also known as an eviction action. “A forcible entry and detainer action is a statutory remedy….” CS/RW Westlake Indoor Storage, L.L.C. v. Kesi, L.L.C. , No.

102535, 2015 WL 6819281, ¶ 13 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Nov. 5, 2015). Accord Egner
v. Egner, 24 Ohio App.3d 171, 172, 493 N.E.2d 999 (11th Dist. 1985). It is a remedy unknown at common law and thus purely statutory.Fodor v. First Nat’l Supermarkets, Inc., 63 Ohio St.3d 489, 494, 589 N.E.2d 17 (1992).
