R.C. 4705.01 prohibits any person who is not an attorney from commencing or maintaining a court action on behalf of another. See Otto v. Patterson , 173 Ohio St.

174, 180 N.E.2d 575 (1962).

A non-attorney agent of a landlord may not sign an eviction complaint on behalf of the landlord. Furr v. Patterson , No. 2021 -CVG-004661 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland,
Hous. Div., June 22, 2021); Wheeler v. Write , No. 2020 -CVG-082W (Ohio County Ct., Ashtabula, W. Div., Apr. 28, 2020); Croxton v. Freisner, 62 Ohio Misc.2d 513, 603 N.E.2d 1162 (Ohio Mun., Canton, 1992); Steiner v. Nadal , No. 91 -CVG-8356 (Ohio Mun., Canton, Apr. 2, 1992); Ogden v. Avery , No. 91 -CVG-18 (Ohio Mun., Mansfield, May 16, 1991); Wright v. Phelps, No. 89-CVGT-1086 (Ohio Mun., Miami Cty., Sept. 18, 1989); Porter v. Gibbs , No. 88 -CVG-205 (Ohio Mun., Jackson Cty., May 31, 1988).
