## Source text

Absent a tenant’s fraud or misrepresentation in the application process, an assisted housing landlord is not permitted to terminate a tenancy based on a tenant’s pre - admission criminal conduct. See Bennington Hous. Aut h. v. Bush , 182 Vt. 133, 933 A.2d 207 (2007); Wellstone Hous. Auth. v. Murphy, 131 S.W.3d 378 (Mo. App. 2004).

See also 66 Fed. Reg. 28776, 28781 (May 24, 2001) (HUD preamble on criminal activity grounds for terminating a tenancy: “Activity occurring only prior to the time the leaseholder signed the lease, or the household member or guest joined the household or became a guest, would not be a basis for termination of tenancy”).
