# 1. Service of Notice to Vacate on the PHA

## Table-of-contents context

- III. Eviction Action, Additional
  - H. Housing Choice Voucher Program Issues

## Source text

In the Housing Choice Voucher program (“HCVP”), a landlord who seeks to evict a tenant is required to serve the public housing authority (“PHA”) with a copy of the R.C. 1923.04 notice to vacate that the landlord serves on the tenant. 24 C.F.R.

§ 982.310(e)(2)(ii). “The owner must give the PHA a copy of any owner eviction notice at the same time the owner notifies the tenant.” HUD Tenancy Addendum, form HUD-52641-A (July 2019), ¶ 11(b).

“It is essential that the PHA be given timely notice of the commencemen t of the proceedings to terminate the tenancy, not only so that it does not continue to make 261 housing subsidy payments on behalf of a tenant who is no longer in possession, but also to enable it to monitor the actions of the landlord and to afford it the op portunity to intervene if it deems it necessary to protect the interest of the Section 8 tenant.” Lamlon Dev. Corp. v. Owens , 141 Misc.2d 287, 294, 533 N.Y.S.2d 186 (Dist. Ct.

1988). Accord Sultanik v. Byrd , 15 Misc.3d 1141(A), 841 N.Y.S.2d 823 (Just. Ct .

2007); Homestead Equities, Inc. v. Washington , 176 Misc.2d 459, 672 N.Y.S.2d 980
(Civ. Ct. 1998).

A landlord’s failure to serve timely the PHA with a copy of the notice to vacate is a defense to the eviction action. 223 Asbury Apts. v. Hibbler , No. 13932 , 1994 WL 579845 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Oct. 19, 1994); Nittskoff v. Sanford, No. 2010 -CVG- 183 (Ohio Mun., South Euclid, May 12, 2010);Cotton v. McQueen, No. CVG-0700796 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland Heights, July 10, 2007); Kosak v. Sadler, No. 04-CVG-5145 (Ohio Mun., Dayton, Sept. 18, 2004); Am. Hous. Foundation v. Bridge, No. 02-CVG- 01157 (Ohio Mun., Middletown, June 20, 2002);Green v. Sephus, No. 90-CVG-1768- 38 (Ohio Mun., East Cleveland, Jan. 8, 1991). See also Winns v. Rosado , 440 N.J.
Super. 98, 111 A.3d 155 (2014); Santouse v. Scott , No. HDSP137470, 2006 WL 1600385 (Conn. Super. May 31, 2006); Zeoli v. Vargas, No. CV-040287703S, 2004 WL 2361544 (Conn. Super. Aug. 19, 2004); Fenelon v. Jones , No. LT -000174/11, 2011 WL 2022631 (N.Y. Dist. Ct. May 24, 2011); Sultanik v. Byrd , 15 Misc.3d 1141(A), 841 N.Y.S.2d 823 (Just. Ct. 2007); Homestead Equities, Inc. v. Washington, 176 Misc.2d 459, 672 N.Y.S.2d 980 (Civ. Ct. 1998). Cf. Lee v. Handel, No. 85-CVG- 6674 (Ohio Mun., Akron, Aug. 30, 1985) (Section 8 Moderate Rehabilitation, lease requirement).
