# f. PTFA – Deed in Lieu of Foreclosure

## Source text

The PTFA does not apply to a deed in lieu of foreclosure because the title has not been transferred by foreclosure. However, a deed inlieu of foreclosure is governed by Ohio common law on the sale of tenant -occupied real property. See Section XXII.B, above.
