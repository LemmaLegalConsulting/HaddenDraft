# b. Deceased Resident of Manufactured Home Park

## Table-of-contents context

## Source text

When the adverse party i n an eviction action is a deceased resident of a manufactured home park, the R.C. 1923.04(A) notice to vacate “shall be left at the premises from which the defendant is sought to be evicted and shall also be sent by ordinary mail to the following persons i f their names and addresses are known to the park operator:” (1) any executor or administrator appointed by the probate court for any estate of the adverse party; and (2) “the deceased resident’s spouse and any other members of the deceased resident’s immediate family.” R.C. 1923.04(C).
