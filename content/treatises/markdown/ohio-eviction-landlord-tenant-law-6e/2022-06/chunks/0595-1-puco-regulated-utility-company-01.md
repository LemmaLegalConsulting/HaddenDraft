# 1. PUCO Regulated Utility Company

## Table-of-contents context

- XXII. Miscellaneous Landlord-Tenant
  - D. Utility Termination for Landlord
    - 1. PUCO Regulated Utility Company

## Source text

When a tenant’s utility services are included in the rental payments and are furnished by a PUCO regulated utility company, PUCO rules provide the tenant with certain safeguards in any termination of service for the landlord’s nonpayment.383 Ohio
Admin. Code § 4901:1-18-08.

The safeguards include the utility comp any’s duty to provide the tenant with ten days advance notice of the proposed termination of service. Id. In the notice, the company must “summarize the remedies tenants may choose to prevent disconnection or to have service reconnected.” Id.

The safeguards also include the tenant’s right to maintain (or reinstate) the utility service by paying the amount due for the current month’s service for the building (plus any reconnection charge). Id.

The safeguards also include, for mastermetered premises, the tenant’s right to maintain (or reinstate) the utility service by timely invoking the R.C. 5321.07 rent deposit procedures, provided at least fifty percent of the tenants of the occupied dwelling units in the building participate. Id. Under this remedy, after rent depositing, the tenants apply to the court for an order to use the rent deposit to maintain or reinstate the utility service. Id.
