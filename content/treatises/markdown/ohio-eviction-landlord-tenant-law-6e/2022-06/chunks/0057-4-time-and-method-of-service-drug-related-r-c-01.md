1923.02(A)(6) Eviction In an eviction action filed under R.C. 1923.02(A)(6)(a) (see Section I.C.5, above), the complaint and summons must be served in accordance with Civil Rules 4.1 and 4.6. R.C. 1923.051(A)(1).38 Furthermore, the service and returnof the summons shall be made, if possible, within three working days after the filing of the complaint. R.C.

1923.051(A)(1).

In these eviction actions, the court must schedule a trial date for the eviction claim that is not later than the thirtieth calen dar day after the date on which the tenant is properly served with the summons. R.C. 1923.051(A)(2).
