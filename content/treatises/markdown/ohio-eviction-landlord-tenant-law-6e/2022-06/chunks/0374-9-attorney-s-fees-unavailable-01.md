# 9. Attorney’s Fees, Unavailable

## Table-of-contents context

- IX. Tenant Claims for Defective
  - A. R.C. 5321.04 Contract Claim
    - 9. Attorney’s Fees, Unavailable

## Source text

In an R.C. 5321.04 contract claim, a tenant may not recover attorney’s fees.

Iwenofu v. Consol. Mgmt., Inc., 49 Ohio App.3d 33, 550 N.E.2d 505 (8th Dist. 1988).

See Section XIX.A, below.

The only R.C. 5321.04 claim in which attorney’s fees are authorized is an R.C.

5321.04(B) claim, which is based on a landlord’s unlawful entry upon the premises.

Iwenofu, 49 Ohio App. 3d at 34-35. For a discussion on those claims, see Section X.B, below.
