# 2. Advance Payment of Last Month’s Rent

## Table-of-contents context

- XVI. Security Deposit
  - B. Distinction Between a Security Deposit
    - 2. Advance Payment of Last Month’s Rent

## Source text

When a rental agreement requires a tenant to pay (and the tenant pays) the last month’s rent at the commencement of the tenancy, the advance payment of the last month’s rent is a security deposit. Hart v. Pervan , No. 79915, 2002 WL 31528735 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Nov. 14, 2002); Y ancey v. Haehn, No. 99 -G-2210, 2000 WL 263757 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Mar. 3, 2000).
