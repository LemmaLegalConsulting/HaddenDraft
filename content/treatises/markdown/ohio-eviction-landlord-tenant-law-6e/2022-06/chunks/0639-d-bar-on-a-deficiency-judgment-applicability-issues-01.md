# d. Bar on a Deficiency Judgment – Applicability Issues

## Source text

When a vendor serves the vendee with an R.C. 5313.06 notice and, thereupon, the vendee vacates the property, the R.C. 5313.10 bar against a deficiency judgment applies, even though the vendor did not file an action for forfeiture. Koehler v.

647 Panizgua, No. 5-02-64, 2003 WL 1903737 (Ohio App., 3rd Dist., Apr. 18, 2003).

When a vendor has a note and mortgage with the vendee, in a ddition to the land installment contract, and the vendor commences a foreclosure action under the note and mortgage (and not under R.C. 5313.07), the R.C. 5313.10 bar against a deficiency judgment does not apply. Bradford v. B & P Wrecking Co., 171 Ohio App.3d 616, 872 N.E.2d 331 (6th Dist. 2007).

When a vendor is seeking to collect payment under a guarantee agreement, the R.C. 5313.10 bar against a deficiency judgment does not apply to the guarantor.

O’Brien v. Ravenswood Apts., Ltd., 169 Ohio App.3d 233, 862 N.E.2d 549 (1st Dist.

2006).
