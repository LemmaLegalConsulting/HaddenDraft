## Source text

Ohio municipalities have enacted ordinances, and Ohio courts have adopted court rules, that provide tenants with rights that supplement their state law rights. Six examples of those ordinances and rules are discussed in Sections XXII.J.2. -7, below.

The areas of tenant rights that they address are:a pay to stay defense in eviction actions, a limit on late payment fees, a right to counsel in eviction actions, sealing records of an eviction action, a source of income protection, and lead safe rental housing.
