“[T]he efforts of a legal intern on behalf of a client isproperly compensable as part of an award of attorney fees.”Jackson v. Brown, 83 Ohio App.3d 230, 232, 614 N.E.2d 847 (8th Dist. 1992). Accord Non-Employees of Chateau Estates Resident Ass’n v.

Chateau Estates, Ltd., Nos. 2004-CA-19,20, 2004 WL 1587234, ¶ 33 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., July 9, 2004).
