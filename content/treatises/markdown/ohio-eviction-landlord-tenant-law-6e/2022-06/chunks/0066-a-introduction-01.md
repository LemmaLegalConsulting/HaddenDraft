## Source text

Two types of advance deposits may be required of a tenant who requests a trial by jury in an eviction action. The first is the advance deposit that a municipal court may require under R.C. 1901.26(A)(3). This deposit is discussed in Section I.J.3.b, below.

The second is an advance deposit of current rent that a court may require, togetherwith an order to deposit subsequent rent as it becomes due, under the court’s inherent power or R.C. 1923.08. This deposit is discussed in Section I.J.3.c, below.
