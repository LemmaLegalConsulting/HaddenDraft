## Source text

When the ground for an eviction action is a tenant’s violation of an R.C.

5321.05(A) obligation that materially affects health and safety, R.C. 1923.02(A)(8) is the source of the court’s subject-matter jurisdiction.

R.C. 1923.02(A)(8) imposes a requirement on any landlord who seeks to invoke this subject-matter jurisdiction: “Prior to the commencem ent of an action under this division, notice shall be given to the tenant and compliance secured with section 5321.11 of the Revised Code.” Therefore, in such eviction actions, the landlord’s compliance with the R.C.

5321.11 notice and cure rules is a jurisdictional requirement. See Woodrose Ltd.
