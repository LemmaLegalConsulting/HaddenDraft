# 4. Thirty-Day Period

## Table-of-contents context

- XVI. Security Deposit

## Source text

A landlord’s thirty-day period to return the security deposit and/or provide notice and an itemization of deductions from the security deposit is not relieved or suspended merely because the landlord has a pending monetary claim against the tenant. Davis
v. Wesolowski, 146 N.E.3d 633, 2020 -Ohio-677, ¶ 22 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., 2020).

The requirement to abide by R.C. 5321.16(B) is “separate and distinct from a landlord’s claim for any unpaid rent orpremises damaged.” Sherwin v. Cabana Club Apts., 70 Ohio App.2d 11, 18, 433 N.E.2d 932 (8th Dist. 1980). Accord Davis v.

Wesolowski, 146 N.E.3d 633, ¶ 22.

When the date on which the tenant returns the keys to the landlord is subsequent to the date on w hich the tenant vacates the premises, “delivery of possession” occurs when the keys are returned. Katzin v. Murad, No. 46553, 1984 WL 7150 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Jan. 5, 1984). Cf. Griffin v. Currie, No. 4065, 1988 WL 126385 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Nov. 25, 1988) (tenant liable to landlord for rent for the month after tenant had vacated because tenant returned the keys on the second day of that month).

When a month-to-month tenant vacates the premises without serving the landlord with an R.C. 5321.17(B) thirty-day notice of termination, the R.C. 5321.16(B) thirty - day period begins to run on the date that the tenant’s obligation to pay rent ends.

Hashemi v. Jim Owen & Co., No. 76AP-879, 1977 Ohio App. LEXIS 8510 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., Apr. 21, 1977).

A landlord satisfies the R.C. 5321.16(B) thirty-day requirement if, on or before the thirtieth day, the landlord mails the security deposit and/or written itemization to the tenant’s forwarding address with proper postage. Goldstein v. Spitz, 30 Ohio App.3d 246, 507 N.E.2d 433 (1st Dist. 1986); Paxton v. McGranahan, No. 49645, 1985 WL 6444 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., 1985). When a landlord timely mails the security deposit and/or written itemization, R.C. 5321.16(B) does not require that the tenant actually receive them within the thirty-day period. Id.
