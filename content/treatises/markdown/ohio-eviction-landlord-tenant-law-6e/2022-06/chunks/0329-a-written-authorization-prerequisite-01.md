## Source text

A landlord may not assess a tenant a fee for late payment of rent without a written agreement between the parties that expressly permits the assessment of the fee.

Neubauer v. Patzkowsky, No. 91AP-1236, 1992 WL 125269 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., June 2, 1992); Moran v. Foster, No. 97 -CVG-300 (Ohio Mun., Lakewood, Dec. 30, 1997).
