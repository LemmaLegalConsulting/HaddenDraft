# 1. Definition of Land Installment Contract

## Table-of-contents context

- XXIII. Land Installment Contracts,
  - A. Introduction
    - 1. Definition of Land Installment Contract

## Source text

The definition of a land installment contract is set forth in R.C. 5313.01(A).Shrock
v. Spognardi, 46 N.E.3d 1115, 2015-Ohio-4555, ¶ 16 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., 2015).

The definition is comprised of three elements: (1) an executory contract that is not required to be fully performed by one or more of the parties within one year; (2) the vendor’s agreement to convey title to real property and the vendee’s agreement to purchase the property and to pay the purchase price in installments; and (3) the vendor’s retention of the title as security for performance. See Am. Servicing Corp. v.

Wannemacher, 19 N.E.3d 566, 2014-Ohio-3984, ¶ 28 (Ohio App., 3rd Dist., 2014).

“[A] land installment contract conveys a present ownership interest in realty.” A & J Homes, Inc. v. Green, No. 101416, 2015 WL 1511020, ¶ 11 (Ohio App., 8th Dist.,
Apr. 2, 2015). Accord Shrock v. Spognardi, 46 N.E.3d 1115, 2015-Ohio-4555, ¶ 15 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., 2015).

“A land installment contract is an executory agreement whereby the purchaser
(vendee) agrees to pay the purchase price and is vested with equitable ownership, while the seller (vendor) retains bare legal title in the property to secure payment of the purchase price.” Baraby v. Swords, 166 Ohio App.3d 527, 851 N.E.2d 559, ¶ 14 (3rd
Dist. 2006). Accord Voska v. Coffman, No. S-13-008, 2013 WL 6579576, ¶ 9 (Ohio App., 6th Dist., Dec. 13, 2013); Judson v. Lyendecker , No. 12AP -615, 2013 WL 1187618, ¶ 8 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., Mar. 21, 2013).
