Section XXIV.B, below, is an index of HUD regulations that govern the primary HUD-assisted rental housing programs. The index identifies, per program, the HUD regulations on: (1) overall program rul es, (2) selection of tenants, (3) leases, (4) rent formula, (5) interim rent reduction, and (6) termination of tenancy and eviction.

Section XXIV.C, below, discusses R.C. Chapter 2506 appeals of a public housing authority’s decision to terminate a famil y’s participation in the Housing Choice Voucher program (“HCVP”).

For a discussion on eviction actions, relative to the HUD -assisted rental housing programs, see Chapter III, above. For a detailed outline of the HUD regulations on terminating a tenancy for the criminal activity of a tenant, household member, or guest, see Section III.G.8, above.

For a comprehensive discussion on the HUD-assisted rental housing programs, see National Housing Law Project, HUD Housing Programs: Tenants’ Rights ( 5th ed.

2018).
