## Source text

County courts do not have equity jurisdiction. Gallagher v. Billmaier , 79 Ohio Law Abs. 417, 154 N.E.2d 472 (Ohio App., 6th Dist., 1958).

361 Two municipal courts have a housing division, Cleveland Municipal Court and Toledo Municipal Court. R.C. 1901.011. One municipal court has an environmental division, Franklin County Municipal Court. Id. The housing and environmental divisions are not subject to the R.C. 1901.17 monetary jurisdictional limit. See Section XX.E, below.
