Prior notice to the landlord is no t a requirement for asserting a claim as a counterclaim under R.C. 1923.061(B). Lobo v. Hoelscher , 44 Ohio Misc. 46, 336 N.E.2d 475 (Ohio Mun., Dayton, 1975). However, prior notice to the landlord may be an element of the underlying claim. See Sections IX.A.2 and IX.B.2, below.
