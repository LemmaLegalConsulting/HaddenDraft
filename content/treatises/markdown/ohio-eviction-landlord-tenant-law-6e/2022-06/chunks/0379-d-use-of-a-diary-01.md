# d. Use of a Diary

## Source text

A tenant’s testimony may be strengthened through the use of a diary. While testifying, a tenant may be permitted to use a writt en diary to refresh the tenant’s
recollection. Ohio R. Evid. 612. E.g., Polk v. Gray , No. 36896, 1978 Ohio App.

LEXIS 9771, at *10-11 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Feb. 9, 1978).

The extent to which a witness may use a written memorandum to help recollect past events is within the sound discretion of the trial court. Polk, 1978 Ohio App.

LEXIS 9771, at *10 -11; Sroufe v. Guttman , 65 Ohio App. 556, 32 N.E.2d 444 (1st
Dist. 1940).
