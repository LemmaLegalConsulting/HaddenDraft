## Source text

The R.C. 5321.17(B) notice must be given at least thirty days before the periodic rental date. R.C. 5321.17(B). See also Risch v. Samuel , No. C -190159, 2020 WL 1488724 (Ohio App., 1st Dist., Mar. 25, 2020);Detweiter v. Galt, No. 01-CA-34, 2001 WL 1682822 (Ohio App., 4th Dist., Dec. 26, 2001); DeBenedictus v. Gialamas, No.

96-L-006, 1997 WL 51453 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Jan. 24, 1997);M. Friedman Mgmt.
Co. v. Malek, No. 93-L-022, 1993 WL 548091 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Dec. 3, 1993);

Dunning v. Green, No. F-82-12, 1983 WL 13825 (Ohio App., 6th Dist., Feb. 4, 1983);

Siegler v. Batdorff, 63 Ohio App.2d 76, 408 N.E.2d 1383 (8th Dist. 1979).

This differs from a thirty-day period before the termination date (Detweiter, 2001 WL 1682822, at *1), and the difference is often significant.

The court decisions set forth below ill ustrate the R.C. 5321.17(B) notice period (i.e., thirty days before the periodic rental date). Each of these court decisions concerned a tenancy with a periodic rental date of the first day of the month, unless another date is specified.
