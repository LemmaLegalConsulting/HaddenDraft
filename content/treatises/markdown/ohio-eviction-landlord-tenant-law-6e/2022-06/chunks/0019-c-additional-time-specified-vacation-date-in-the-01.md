## Source text

Notice If the notice to vacate states a date by which the tenant is to vacate the premises and it affords the tenant more time than the minimum three -day period under R.C.

1923.04(A), the landlord may not file the eviction action until after the vacation date specified in the notice. Wodzisz v. Bayes, No. 96-APE07-891, 1997 WL 142715, at *5 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., Mar. 25, 1997). But see Crawford v. Norman , 46 Ohio Law
Abs. 404, 66 N.E.2d 559 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., 1946); Mack v. Eckerlin, 2 Ohio L.R.

441 (Ohio App., 1st Dist., 1905), aff’d, 75 Ohio St. 627 (1907).
