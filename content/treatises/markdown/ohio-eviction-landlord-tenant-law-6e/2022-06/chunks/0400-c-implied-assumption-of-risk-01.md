# c. Implied Assumption of Risk

## Source text

The defense of implied assumption of risk is not available as an absolute bar to an R.C. 5321.04 negligence per se claim. Anderson v. Ceccardi, 6 Ohio St.3d 110, 451 N.E.2d 780 (1983). See also Crawford v. Wolfe, No. 01-CA-2811, 2002 WL 31521593 (Ohio App., 4th Dist., Sept. 26, 2002). Under the comparative negligence statute (R.C.

2315.19), the defense of implied assumption of risk is merged with the defense of contributory negligence. Id.
