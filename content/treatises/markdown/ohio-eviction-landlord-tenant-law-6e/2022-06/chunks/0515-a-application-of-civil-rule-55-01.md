## Source text

When a landlord’s complaint includes an eviction claim and a monetary claim for property damage (or unpaid rent), the tenant’s presence at the eviction hearing constitutes an appearance in the action for Civil Rule 55(A) purposes. For a discussion on this issue, see Section XVII.D.1.a, above.
