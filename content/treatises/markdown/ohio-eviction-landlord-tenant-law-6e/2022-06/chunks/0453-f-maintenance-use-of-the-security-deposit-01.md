# F. Maintenance/Use of the Security Deposit,

## Table-of-contents context

- XVI. Security Deposit

## Source text

During the Tenancy R.C. Chapter 5321 does not impose any restrictions or requirements on a landlord’s maintenance or use of a secur ity deposit during the tenancy. Mustic v. Mustic , No.

87AP-318, 1987 WL 32174 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., Dec. 29, 1987).

For example, it does not require a landlord to maintain a separate account for the security deposit. Shoregate Towers Partners L.L.C. v. Antebi, No. 109817, 2021 WL 3416980, ¶ 128 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Aug. 5, 2021) (“We therefore agree with appellants that there is no legal duty to segregate tenants’ security deposits by themselves in a separate account”); Mustic v. Mustic, No. 87AP-318, 1987 WL 32174 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., Dec. 29, 1987) . And it does not require a landlord to use a tenant’s security deposit to pay the tenant’s rent arrearage so as to avoid the tenant’s default on the lease. Small v. Williams, No. 44418, 1982 WL 5247 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Mar. 25, 1982).
