# g. Misdemeanor

## Source text

If the criminal activity is a misdemeanor, it may be a minor violation of the lease.

E.g., Denhart v. Whaley, No. 88-CV-16092 (Ohio Mun., Hamilton Cty., Nov. 2, 1988) (attempted promotion of prostitution). See also Hous. Auth. of Decatur v. Brown, 180
Ga. App. 483, 349S.E.2d 501 (1986) (possession of less than one ounce of marijuana).

A minor violation of an assisted housing lease is not good cause to terminate the lease, unless there are repeated minor violations. See Section III.G.12, below.
