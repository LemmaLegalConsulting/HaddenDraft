# 5. Enforcement of R.C. 5313.02(A) Requirements

## Table-of-contents context

- XXIII. Land Installment Contracts,
  - B. Contract Requirements and Related
    - 5. Enforcement of R.C. 5313.02(A) Requirements

## Source text

If a vendor fails to comply with an R.C. 5313.02(A) requirement, the vendee may file an action to enforce it. R.C. 5313.04. Upon finding such noncompliance, “the court shall grant appropriate relief.” Id. E.g., Labbe v. Smith, No. 11078, 1983 WL 3899 (Ohio App., 9th Dist., Nov. 2, 1983) (in eviction for nonpayment, court enforced vendee’s R.C. 5313.02(A)(13) rights by granting vendee credit for his payments to vendor’s mortgagee).

However, “[t]here is no provision in the statute [R.C. Chapter 5313] for a remedy in damages for noncompliance.” 393 Akron First Seventh Day Adventist Church v.

Smith, No. 11577, 1984 WL 3956, at *2 (Ohio App., 9th Dist., Sept. 27, 1984).
