# 6. Landlord Divested of Ownership

## Table-of-contents context

- XVII. Landlord Claim for Rent
  - A. End Date of Rental Obligation

## Source text

The tenant’s rental obligation to a landlord ends on the date that the landlord is divested of ownership of the premises. Tillimon v. Moore, Nos. L-18-1040,1041, 2018 WL 3814842 (Ohio App., 6th Dist., Aug. 10 , 2018). For example, when a sheriff’s sale in a foreclosure action divests a landlord of ownership of the premises, the tenant’s rental obligation to the landlord ends as of the date of that sale. Id.
