When the tenant is a party in the foreclosure action, the confirmation of t he foreclosure sale terminates the tenancy. See Prudential Ins. Co. of Am. v. Bull Market, Inc., 66 Ohio Misc. 9, 420 N.E.2d 140 (C.P., Montgomery Cty., 1979); Davis v.

Boyajian, 11 Ohio Misc. 97, 229 N.E.2d 116 (C.P., Stark Cty., 1967).

In these circumst ances, the foreclosure court may issue an order for the forcible removal of the tenant from the premises. See Ohio Att’y Gen. Op. No. 78-015 (Apr.

When the tenant is not a party in the foreclosure action, recent appellate court decisions hold t hat the confirmation of the foreclosure sale still terminates the tenancy.379 Victoria Mortgage Corp. v.
