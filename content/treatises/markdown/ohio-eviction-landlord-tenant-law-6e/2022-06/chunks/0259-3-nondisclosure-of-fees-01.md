# 3. Nondisclosure of Fees

## Table-of-contents context

- IV. Eviction Action, Additional
  - C. Additional Defenses
    - 3. Nondisclosure of Fees

## Source text

A park operator is required to disclose, in writing, to each mh park owner and to each mh park tenant, all of the fees, including rental fees, that the park operator proposes to charge the owner or tenant. R.C. 4781.40(B) (former R.C. 3733.11(B)).

The park operator is required to make this disclosure before the owner (or tenant) executes a rental agreement andbefore the owner (or tenant) assumes occupancy in the mh park. Id.

The park operator is also required to provide each mh park owner and each mh park tenant with at least thirty days advance written notice of any change in these fees.

Id.

A park operator’s noncompliance with these disclosure requirements bars the park operator from assessing or collecting any fee not so disclosed ( see R.C.

4781.40(B),(H)(4) (former R.C. 3733.11(B),(H)(4)) and from evicting the owner or tenant for nonpayment of any fee not so disclosed (see R.C. 4781.40(B)). E.g., May v.

Petrick, No. 2009-CA-00031, 2010 WL 1452604 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., Apr. 8, 2010);

Callahan v. Croxford, No. 2006-CVF-00621, 2007 WL 4359907 (Ohio Mun., Athens Cty., July 31, 2007); Reck v. Lucas, No. 2006-CVG-00235, 2006 WL 4399761 (Ohio Mun., Auglaize Cty., Aug. 25, 2006);Bennett v. Croxford, No. 96-CVH-000488, 1997 WL 810044 (Ohio Mun., Athens Cty., Apr. 18, 1997); Evangelinos v. Nixon, No. 95- CVG-00076, 1997 WL 810049 (Ohio County Ct., Belmont C ty., Jan. 11, 1997). For additional court decisions on this issue, see Section XXI.C.4.d (disclosure of fee/rent), below.

In an eviction action, any alleged failure to meet this disclosure requirement is an affirmative defense for which the mh park owner or mh park tenant has the burden of
proof. Harrison Parks, Inc. v. Bozarth, No. 20147, 2004 WL 917333 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Apr. 30, 2004); Dearwester’s Gem City Estates v. Nelson, No. 14320, 1994 WL 681104 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Dec. 7, 1994).
