# F. R.C. 5321.12 Claim

## Table-of-contents context

- IX. Tenant Claims for Defective
  - F. R.C. 5321.12 Claim

## Source text

“Under R.C. 5321.12, a party has a statutory remedy for damages for the breach of any duty imposed under the Act [R.C. Chapter 5321].” Sherman v. Pearson, 110 Ohio App.3d 70, 74, 673 N.E.2d 643 (1st Dist. 1996).

However, with respect to R.C. 5321.04(A) violations, courts have not used R.C.

5321.12 as authority to provide tenants with any claim or remedy outside the R.C.

5321.04 contract claim (see Section IX.A, above) and the R.C. 5321.04 negligence per se claim (see Section IX.B, above).
