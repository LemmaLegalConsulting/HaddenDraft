# b. Application of Civil Rule 54(C)

## Table-of-contents context

## Source text

When a landlord’s complaint includes an eviction claim and a monetary claim for property damage (or unpaid rent), Civil Rule 54(C) applies to the monetary claim. For a discussion on this issue, see Section XVII.D.1.b, above.
