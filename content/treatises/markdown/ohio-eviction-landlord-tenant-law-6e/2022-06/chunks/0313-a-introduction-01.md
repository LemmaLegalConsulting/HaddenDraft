## Source text

A rental agreement may impose tenant obligations regarding maintenance or use of the premises, in addition to the R.C. 5321.05(A) obligations. R.C. 5321.06.

However, those obligations may not be “in consistent with or prohibited by Chapter 5321 of the Revised Code or any other rule of law. Id.
