# 5. Forwarding Address

## Source text

LEXIS 13428 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., Nov. 13, 1980) (untimely where served after the R.C. 5321.16(B) thirty-day period had expired).

The tenant’s compliance (or noncompliance) with the R.C. 5321.16(B) requirement to provide a forwarding address determines whether the tenant may recover statutory damages and reasonable attorney’s fees under R.C. 5321.16(C), but not whether the tenant may recover the security deposit. See Section XVI.H.1, below.
