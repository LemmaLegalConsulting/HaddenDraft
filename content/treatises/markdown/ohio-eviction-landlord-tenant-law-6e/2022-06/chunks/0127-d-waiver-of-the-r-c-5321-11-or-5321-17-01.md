With respect to the landlord’s acceptance of future rent after the expiration of the thirty-day period, an alternative arg ument (in lieu of waiver) is a new tenancy
argument. Its basic premise is that the landlord’s acceptance of future rent creates a new periodic tenancy, which gives the tenant the right to remain in possession of the premises notwithstanding the terminatio n of the prior tenancy.
