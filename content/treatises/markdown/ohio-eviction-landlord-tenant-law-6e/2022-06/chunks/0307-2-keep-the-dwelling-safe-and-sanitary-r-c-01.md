# 2. Keep the Dwelling Safe and Sanitary, R.C.

## Source text

5321.05(A)(1) R.C. 5321.05(A)(1) generally requires a tenant to keep the dwelling unit safe and
sanitary. Sanitary means clean; it does not mean free of mess. Vesta Cleveland LLC
v. Swann, No. 2003-CVG-5468 (Ohio Mun., Clevela nd, Hous. Div., May 20, 2003).

For example, piles of clothing are messy, not unclean. Id.

Also, “R.C. 5321.05(A)(1) applies only to those situations in which the tenant is occupying and using the leased premises.” Cent. Mut. Ins. Co. v. Faller , 71 Ohio Misc.2d 58, 61, 654 N.E.2d 213 (Ohio C.P., Licking Cty., 1995). Therefore, it does not apply to a co-tenant who is not in possession or control of the premises. Id.
