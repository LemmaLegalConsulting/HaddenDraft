Damron v. McKnight, No. 03-CVG-524 (Ohio Mun., Chillicothe, Dec. 12, 2003) R.C. 5321.04 violation (manufactured home): windows (most did not open and algae growth around some); kitchen (sink leak and weakened flooring); living room (mold on carpet due to window leaks, and bare electrical wires hanging from ceiling);

bedrooms (ceiling sinking, with evidence of mold growth, and floors weak and sinking); bathroom (flooring extremely weak and rotting due to bathtub and sink leaks, and hole to exterior in closet). Contract rent: $200 per month. Damage award (settlement): total of $800 for 6.5 months (based on ren t reduction from $1,300 to $500).

Pierson-Kalabins v. Shaw , No. CV2003 -12-3449, 2003 WL 25780617 (Ohio C.P., Butler Cty., Feb. 11, 2003) R.C. 5321.04 violation: lack of reasonable heat; “live wires coming from the ceiling in the kitchen, an unfinished wal l between the kitchen and the living room, several unfinished electrical outlets throughout the home, unfinished insulation in the back patio, several windows that were unframed, and broken plumbing fixtures in the bathroom;” and “roof was unfinished, and,as a result, creatures were crawling up there all of the time.” Contract rent: $1,000 per month. Damage award (lack of reasonable heat): $200 per month for four months, total of $800. Damage award (other defective conditions): $300 per month for 16 months, total of $4,800.

Parker v. Hiles, No. 02-CVH-362 (Ohio Mun., Chillicothe, Jan. 6, 2003) R.C. 5321.04 violation: “the rental unit ... was in poor condition resulting in condemnation.” City inspector’s report found broken windows, exposed wiring, holes in walls and floors, bathroom sink unable to drain, bathtub sinking, basement full of trash and solid waste, electric or kerosene heaters due to lack of gas, and more.

Contract rent: $300 per month. Damage award: $225 per month for seven months, total of $1,575.

Williams v. Wright, No. 2000-CVG-01040 (Ohio Mun., Marietta, Aug. 15, 2001) R.C. 5321.04 violation: roach infestation. Contract rent: $375 per month. Damage award: $225 per month for five months, total of $1,125.

Woods v. Coleman, No. 01-CVG-444 (Ohio Mun., Jackson Cty., Aug. 6, 2001) R.C. 5321.04 violation: no running water or working toilet. Contract rent: $350 per
month. Damage award: $300 per month for six months, total of $1,800.

Malcom v. Tate , No. 99 -CVH-21689 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, Hou s. Div., June 29, 2001) R.C. 5321.04 violation: “electrical problems, backyard containing garbage and debris, window to the back door missing, lack of working stove, stains on the living room carpet, hole in the hallway floor, mildew on the bathroom wall and ceiling, part of the baseboard in the bathroom missing, several heat register covers missing, no locks on bedroom windows, doorknob on side entry door missing, doorknob on second bedroom door missing, inoperative ceiling fan in dining room, gutter on si de of house missing, and living room windows which would not open.” Contract rent: $369 per month.

Damage award: $100 per month for five months, total of $500.

413 Hansgen v. Seigel, No. 00-CVR-40822 (Ohio Mun., Franklin Cty., May 29, 2001) R.C. 5321.04 viol ation: no water or heat except for portable heater in one room.

Contract rent: not specified. Damage award: the entire contract rent per month for five months.

O’Connor v. Maynard, No. 00-CVG-01 (Ohio Mun., Jackson Cty., Apr. 17, 2001) R.C. 5321.04 violation: defective stove and refrigerator. Contract rent: $275 per
month. Damage award: $50 per month for six months, total of $300.

Tolliver v. Warren, No. 99-CVG-13123 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, Hous. Div., Dec. 28, 1999) R.C. 5321.04 violation: “bathroom floor was cracked, the toilet was not secured to floor, the tub was worn, the sink was not properly affixed to wall, and the ceiling had a hole in it,” defective electrical outlets, a heating vent was not secured to the wall, a screen door was missing a bottom panel, and defect in window seat drawers. Contract rent: $300 per month. Damage award: $75 per month for 12 months, total of $900.

Roundtree v. Valentine, No. 98-CVR-23790 (Ohio Mun., Franklin Cty., Jan. 28, 1999) R.C. 5321.04 violation:47 unspecified code violations and, during the last two months, no hot water and raw sewage/standing water in the basement. Contract rent: $650 per
month. Damage award: $325 per month for four months and $650 per month for the last two months.

Helm v. Peoples, No. 94-CVG-144 (Ohio Mun., Jackson Cty., Apr. 17, 1996) R.C. 5321.04 violation: leaking water lines, improper venting of heaters, lack of heat in portion of house, and improper drainage from house. Contract rent: $250 per month.

Damage award: total of $1,400 for seven months.

Weingarden v. Eagle Ridge Condos., 71 Ohio Misc.2d 7, 653 N.E.2d 759 (Ohio Mun., Toledo, Hous. Div., 1995) R.C. 5321.04 violation: substantial leaking into carpeted basement, whenever rain or snow melting, caused soaked car pet and mildew and, thus, rendered basement (one - third of the apartment) uninhabitable. Contract rent: $750 per month. Damage award:

$250 per month for three months, total of $750.

Crooks v. Kingery, No. 94-CVG-495 (Ohio Mun., Chillicothe, June 14, 1995) R.C. 5321.04 violation: “bathroom floor was noticeably wet and unsanitary, the shower failed to drain properly, the kitchen and bathroom sinks failed to drain properly, and a sewage pipe outside the house discharged sewage onto the ground resulting in an unsanitary condition and offensive smells.” Contract rent: $235 per month. Damage award: total of $770 for seven months.

Myricks v. Reed , No. 93 -CVG-05580 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, Hous. Damage award: $75 per month for nine months, total of $675.

$225 per month for five months, total of $1,125.

Bierman v. Maharidge, No. CVG-920275 (Ohio Mun., Vermilion, Mar. 31, 1993) R.C. 5321.04 violation: no heat to second floor bedrooms, no running water, basement constantly flooded, and back door and porch defective. Contract rent: $200 per month.

Damage award: $100 per month for 17 months, total of $1,700.

Contract rent: not specified. Damage award:

total of $1,175 for 18 months.
