# 5. Subtenant Issues

## Source text

“There is no privity of contract between the original lessor and a subtenant.” City of Cleveland v. A.J. Rose Mfg. Co. , 89 Ohio App.3d 267, 271, 624 N.E.2d 245 (8th
Dist. 1993). Accord Mark-It Place Foods, Inc. v. New Plan Excel Realty Trust, Inc. , 156 O hio App.3d 65, 804 N.E.2d 979, ¶ 19 (4th Dist. 2004). Consequently, “a subtenant cannot maintain an action against the lessor on the original lease.” Id.

Likewise, “the lessor may not sue the subtenant for breach of a covenant contained in the original lease.” Id. However, the lessor may maintain an eviction action against a subtenant. Id. See Section I.O.3, above.
