# 10. Waiver Issues

## Table-of-contents context

- IX. Tenant Claims for Defective
  - A. R.C. 5321.04 Contract Claim

## Source text

“The Landlords and Tenants Act prohibits ... any agreement that purports to waive the tenant’s right or ability to bring an action for damages. R.C. 5321.13; 5321.06.” Miller v. Ritchie, 45 Ohio St. 3d 222, 225, 543 N.E.2d 1265 (1989).

A tenant’s knowledge of the defective condition before the tenant moves into the premises does not constitute a waiver of the tenant’s right to bring an R.C. 5321.04 contract claim. Brown v. August, No. 01-CA-0024, 2002 WL 22877 (Ohio App., 9th Dist., Jan. 9, 2002); Pierson-Kalabins v. Shaw , No. CV2003 -12-3449, 2003 WL 25780617 (Ohio C.P., Butler Cty., Feb. 11, 2003).

A tenant’s occupancy of defective premises over a lengthy period of time does not constitute a waiver of the tenant’s right to bring an R.C. 5321.04 contract claim.Miller
v. Ritchie, 45 Ohio St.3d 222, 224 -26, 543 N.E.2d 1265 (1989). See also Brown v.

August, No. 01 -CA-0024, 2002 WL 22877 (Ohio App., 9th Dist., Jan. 9, 2002) (applying Miller); Kiser v. Stevens, No. 92-CA-54, 1993 WL 35668 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., Feb. 1, 1993) (applying Miller); Pierson-Kalabins v. Shaw , No. CV2003 -12- 3449, 2003 WL 25780617 (Ohio C.P., Butler Cty., Feb. 11, 2003) (applying Miller).

A tenant’s non-use of the R.C. 5321.07 rent deposit remedy does not constitute a waiver of the tenant’s right to bring an R.C. 5321.04 contract claim, irrespective of whether the tenant pays or fails to pay the rent to the landlord. Miller v. Ritchie, 45 Ohio St.3d 222, 224-26, 543 N.E.2d 1265 (1989). See also Boice v. Emshoff, No. 13- 98-23, 1998 WL 833686 (Ohio App., 3rd Dist., Dec. 3, 1998) (applyingMiller); Reiter
v. Fultz, No. 92 -CA-1555, 1993 WL 235522 (Ohio App., 4th Dist., June 30, 1993) (applying Miller). See also Arndt v. P & M Ltd., 163 Ohio App.3d 179, 837 N.E. 398, ¶¶ 23,24 (11th Dist. 2005) (construing R.C. 3733.10,.12 (now R.C. 4781.38,.41) – manufactured home park provisions that are similar to R.C. 5321.04,.07).
