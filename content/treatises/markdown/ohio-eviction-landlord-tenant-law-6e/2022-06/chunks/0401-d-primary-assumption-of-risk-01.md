# d. Primary Assumption of Risk

## Source text

The defense of primary assumption of the risk is not available as a defense in an R.C. 5321.04 negligence per se claim because the claim is based on the landlord’s 445 violation of a statutory duty (R.C. 5321.04(A)). Glover v. Massey, Nos. 56351,56802, 1990 WL 1328 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Jan. 11, 1990).
