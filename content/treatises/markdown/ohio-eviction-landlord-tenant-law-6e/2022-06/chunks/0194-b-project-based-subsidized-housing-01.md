In project-based subsidized housing, the time period for the notice depends on the ground for the termination of tenancy.186 For nonpayment of rent cases, under HUD rules, there are two alternative time periods.

One is a thirty-day period, which applies during national emergencies, when HUD determines that additional time is required for families to secure available funding.

In cases of violations of the lease or applicable federal, state, or local law, the t ime period is determined by state and local law. Id. In “other good cause” cases, the time period is thirty
