## Source text

The counterclaim required by R.C. 1923.061(B) must be filed in accordance with the Ohio Rules of Civil Procedure and, therefore, must be filed in writing. Northgate
Apts. v. Gonzalez, No. CA80-34, 1981 WL 6340 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., June 26, 1981);

Clark Props. v. Hawk, No. 76AP-490, 1976 Ohio App. LEXIS 8193 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., Dec. 21, 1976).
