## Source text

In certain circumstances, courts will enter an order that seals the reco rds of an eviction action. Courts have entered those orders under Rule 45 (E) of the Rules of Superintendence for the Courts of Ohio, under local court rules, and under the court’s inherent authority . These bases for sealing the records of an eviction act ion are discussed in Sections XXII.J.5.b-d, below.
