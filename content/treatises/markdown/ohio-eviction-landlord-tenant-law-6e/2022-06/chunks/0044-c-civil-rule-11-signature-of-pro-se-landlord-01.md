# c. Civil Rule 11 – Signature of Pro Se Landlord

## Source text

When a pro se landlord files an FED complaint, the landlord is required under Civil Rule 11 to sign the complaint. The failure of a pro se landlord to sign the complaint is grounds for the court to dismiss the complaint. E.g., Bennett v. Reynolds, No. CVG- 2100164 (Ohio Mun., Perry Cty., May 19, 2021).
