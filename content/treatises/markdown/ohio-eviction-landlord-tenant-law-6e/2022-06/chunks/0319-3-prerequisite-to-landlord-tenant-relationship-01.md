# 3. Prerequisite to Landlord-Tenant Relationship

## Table-of-contents context

## Source text

An obligation to pay rent, in one of the recognized forms (see Section VII.F.2, above), is a prerequisite to the existence of a landlord -tenant relationship. Mercer v.

Halmbacher, 44 N.E.3d 1011, 2015-Ohio-4167 (Ohio App., 9th Dist., 2015); Lee v.

Wallace, 186 Ohio App.3d 18, 926 N.E.2d 328 (8th Dist. 2010);Amick v. Sickles, 177 Ohio App.3d 337, 894 N.E.2d 733 (4th Dist. 2008); Stone v. Cazeau , No. 07 -CA- 009164, 2007 WL 4146777 (Ohio App., 9th Dist., Nov. 26, 2007); Quigg v. Mullins,
No. L-89-314, 1991 WL 59886 (Ohio App., 6th Dist., Apr. 19, 1991).
