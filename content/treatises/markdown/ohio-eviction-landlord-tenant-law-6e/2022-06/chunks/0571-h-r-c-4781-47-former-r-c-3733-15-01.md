## Source text

Arndt v. P & M Ltd. , Nos. 2007 -P-0038,0039 2008 WL 2042831 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., May 9, 2008) “[A] manufactured home park operator may not avoid the statutory duties imposed by R.C. 3733.10(A) by the inclusion of an ‘as is’ in the lease agreement. ‘No provision of sections 3733.09 to 3733.20 of the Revised Code may be modified or waived by any oral or written agreement,’ except that the ‘[t]he park operato r may agree to assume responsibility for fulfilling any duty or obligation imposed on a resident by section 3733.101 [3733.10.1] of the Revised Code.”
