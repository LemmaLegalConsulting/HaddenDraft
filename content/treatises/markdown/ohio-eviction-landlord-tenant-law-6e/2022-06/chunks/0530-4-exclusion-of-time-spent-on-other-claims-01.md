# 4. Exclusion of Time Spent on Other Claims

## Table-of-contents context

  - F. Standards for Computing the Amount of

## Source text

When an action includes one or more claims for which attorney’s fees may be awarded under R.C. Chapter 5321, and one or more other claims for which attorney’s fees cannot be awarded under R.C. Chapter 5321, the computation of the attorney fee award under R.C. Chapter 5321 is based solely on the time spent on the R.C. Chapter 5321 claims for which attorney’s fees may be awarded. See Section X.B, above (R.C.

5321.04(B) claim); Section XII.B.2, above (R.C. 5321.15(C) claim); Section XVI.H.4, above (R.C. 5321.16(C) claim); Section XVIII.D, above (R.C. 5321.05(C) claim).
