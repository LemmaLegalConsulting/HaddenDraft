# b. Guarantor of Lease

## Source text

A guarantor of a tenant’s lease obligation to pay rent is not an indispensable party, under Civil Rule 19(A), to the landlord’s claim for rent against the tenant. Fed.

Knitting Mills Ltd. v. Harper , 157 Ohio Misc.2d 42, 930 N.E.2d 879 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, 2010).

The tenant and the guarantor are jointly and severally liable. Id. A landlord’s judgment against a tenant on a rent claim is not res judicata against a guarantor, when the landlord has not made the guarantor a party to the action. Id.

However, the guarantor has a due process right to notice and hearing on the underlying unpaid rent claim, before being held liable for any unpaid rent. Id. A lease provision that waives the guarantor’s right to notice of the tenant’s rent delinquency does not waive this due process notice and hearing right. Id.
