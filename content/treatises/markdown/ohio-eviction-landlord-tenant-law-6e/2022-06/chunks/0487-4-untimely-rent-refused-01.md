# 4. Untimely Rent Refused

## Table-of-contents context

- XVII. Landlord Claim for Rent
  - C. Defense Issues
    - 4. Untimely Rent Refused

## Source text

A landlord’s prior refusal to accept an untimely tender of rent does not waive the landlord’s right to assert a claim for that rent. Korom v. Pesek, No. 57559, 1990 WL 130909 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Sept. 13, 1990); Real Prop. Servs. Corp. v. Bonen, No.

93-CVG-7804 (Ohio Mun., Marion Cty., May 26, 1993).
