## Source text

When the vendor asserts these damage claims in a foreclosure action, the court should conduct an evidentiary hearing on the claim after the judicial sale. Flora v.

Pullins, No. 96-CA-13, 1996 WL 648353 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Nov. 8, 1996).

When the vendor brings an eviction action, the vendor may assert the damage claim in the eviction action or in a separate and subsequent action. Marvin v. Stemen, 68 Ohio App.2d 26, 426 N.E.2d 205 (6th Dist. 1980). Res judicata is not a defense to the subsequent action. Id.
