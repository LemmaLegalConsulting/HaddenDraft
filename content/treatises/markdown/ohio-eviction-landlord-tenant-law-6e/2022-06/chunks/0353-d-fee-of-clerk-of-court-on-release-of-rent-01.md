# D. Fee of Clerk of Court on Release of Rent

## Table-of-contents context

- VIII. Rent Depositing and Related
  - D. Fee of Clerk of Court on Release of Rent

## Source text

Deposits, R.C. 5321.08(D) When releasing rent deposits to the landlord or the tenant, the clerk of court may assess a fee for the clerk’s costs relative to the rent deposits. R.C. 5321.08(D). The amount of this fee may be up to one per cent of the amount of the rent deposited. Id.

See also State ex rel. Gudzinas v. Constantino , 43 Ohio App.3d 52, 539 N.E.2d 1 73 (11th Dist. 1988) (finding that a $25 minimum fee violated R.C. 5321.08(D)).

The clerk deducts this fee from the rent deposits when the clerk releases the deposits, regardless of the basis for the release.

This fee is different than the costs that a cou rt may assess under R.C. 5321.09(D) (see Section VIII.E, below). See Smith v. Vilk, No. 46759, 1983 WL 2902 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Dec. 15, 1983) (construing R.C. 3733.121,.122 (now R.C. 4781.42,.43)– the manufactured home park provisions on the clerk’sduties and on releasing rent deposits that are essentially identical to R.C. 5321.08,.09).
