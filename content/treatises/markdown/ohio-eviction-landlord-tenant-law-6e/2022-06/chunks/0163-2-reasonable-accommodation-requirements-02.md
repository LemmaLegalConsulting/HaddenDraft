## Source text

173 Manor Park Apts., LLC v. Garrison , No. 2004-L-029, 2005 WL 940871 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Apr. 22, 2005) (reversed judgment for unpaid rent on ground that tenant was entitled to accommodation to vacate in middle of one year lease, where unit caused tenant to suffer claustrophobia and landlord denied transfer request). See generally Reid v. Plainsboro Partners, III, Nos. 09AP-442,446, 2010 WL 3610931 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., Sept. 16, 2010) (R.C. 4112(I) retaliation claim for rent incr ease and eviction).
