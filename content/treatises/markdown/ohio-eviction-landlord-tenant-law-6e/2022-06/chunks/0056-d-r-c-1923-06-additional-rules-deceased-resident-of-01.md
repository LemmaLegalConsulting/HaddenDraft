## Source text

Manufactured Home Park When the adverse party in an eviction action is a deceased resident of a manufactured home park, the plaintiff must provide the clerk of court with certain information from the probate court (where applicable) or certain information on the deceased resident’s spouse and any other members of the deceased resident’s immediate family, if known to the plaintiff. R.C. 1923.06(F),(I).

If the plaintiff provides the information from the probate cour t, the clerk of court is required to serve the executor or administrator with a copy of the summons and complaint by certified mail, return receipt requested. Id.

If the plaintiff provides the information on the spouse or other immediate family members, the clerk of court is required to serve them with a copy of the summons and complaint by certified mail, return receipt requested, by ordinary mail, and by publication in a newspaper of general circulation. Id.
