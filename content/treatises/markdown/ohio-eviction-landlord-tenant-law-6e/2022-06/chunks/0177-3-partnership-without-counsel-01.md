# 3. Partnership Without Counsel

- II. Eviction Action, Common Defenses
  - Z. Lease, Landlord Prevents Performance

## Source text

A partnership, like a corporation, may not commence or maintain a court action without an attorney. Rasberry v. Taylor, No. 26510, 2013 WL 2327020, ¶ 11 (Ohio App., 9th Dist., May 29, 2013) (dicta).

A non-attorney limited partner of a partnership may not commence or maintain an eviction action on behalf of the partnership without an attorney. See Lakeside Ave.
Ltd. P’ship v. Cuyahoga County Bd. of Revision, 85 Ohio St.3d 125, 707 N.E.2d 472 (1999); Hicks Roofing Co. v. Shah, Shah & Patel Inv. Co., Nos. 88AP-100085,020025, 1989 WL 63323 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., May 16, 1989).

A non-attorney general or managing partner of a partnership may not commence or maintain an eviction action on behalf of the partnership without an attorney. See Hicks Roofing Co. v. Shah, Shal & Patel Inv. Co. , Nos. 88AP-100085,020025, 1989 WL 63323 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., May 16, 1989 ). See also In re ICLNDS Notes Acquisition, LLC , 259 B.R. 289 (Bankr. N.D. Ohio 2001). But see Reed v. BDS Holdings, No. 14-92-41, 1993 WL 195072 (Ohio App., 3rd Dist., June 4, 1993) (held partner permitted to appear on behalf of partnership; partner appeared pro se but court did not address unauthorized practice of law issue).161 A non -attorney employee of a partnership may not commence or maintain an eviction action on behalf of the partnership without an attorney. Johnson Props. v.

Leasure, No. R96-CVG-2566 (Ohio Mun., Portage Cty., Jan. 3, 1997); Sawburg Apts.
v. Magee, No. 87-CV-GF-707 (Ohio Mun., Alliance, June 26, 1987).

A non-attorney spouse of a partner of a partnership may not commence or maintain an eviction action on behalf of the partnership w ithout an attorney. Bishop Kandel Realty v. Decker, No. 89-CVG-1406 (Ohio Mun., Findlay, Apr. 2, 1990).

If a partnership files or maintains an eviction action without an attorney, it is grounds to dismiss the action. See Lakeside Ave. Ltd. P’ship v. Cuyahoga County Bd.

of Revision, 85 Ohio St.3d 125, 707 N.E.2d 472 (1999); Hicks Roofing Co. v. Shah, Shah & Patel Inv. Co., Nos. 88AP-100085,020025, 1989 WL 63323 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., May 16, 1989); In re ICLNDS Notes Acquisition, LLC , 259 B.R. 289 (Bankr.

N.D. Ohio 2001).
