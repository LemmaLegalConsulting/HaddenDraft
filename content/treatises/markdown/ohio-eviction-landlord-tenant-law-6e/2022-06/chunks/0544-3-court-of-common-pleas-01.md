# 3. Court of Common Pleas

## Table-of-contents context

- XX. Jurisdiction
  - B. Monetary Jurisdiction
    - 3. Court of Common Pleas

## Source text

Courts of common pleas have original jurisdiction in civil actions in which the sums or matter in dispute exceed the exclusive original jurisdiction of county courts, currently five hundred dollars. R.C. 2305.01.

A court of common pleas may transfer to a municipal court any civil action in which the amount sought by the plaintiff does not exceed one thousand dollars, provided the municipal court has jurisdiction over t he subject-matter and the parties, and provided the municipal court concurs in the transfer. Id.
