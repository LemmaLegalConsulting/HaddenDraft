# L. Prohibited Occupancy – Sex Offenders,

## Table-of-contents context

## Source text

R.C. 2950.034, 5321.051 R.C. 2950.034 (former R.C. 2950.031) prohibits certain sex offenders from residing within 1,000 feet of any school premises or preschool or child day-care center.

The relevant terms for this prohibition are defined in R.C. 2950.034(C).

R.C. 5321.051 obligates tenants not to permit any such sex offender to occupy the tenant’s rental premises, if the premises are within 1,000 feet of any school premises or preschool or child day-care center. The relevant terms for this obligation are defined in R.C. 5321.01(L) -(N). A tenant’s violation of this obligation is grounds for the landlord to terminate the rental agreement. R.C. 5321.051(A)(2). See also R.C.

1923.01(C)(7),(8), 1923.02(A)(15), 1923.02(C).

R.C. 2950.034 and R.C. 5321.051 apply only to rental agreements that were entered into on or after July 31, 2003. Am. Sub. S.B. No. 5, 125th General Assembly, § 8. See Hyle v. Porter, 117 Ohio St.3d 165, 882 N.E.2d 899 (2008) (R.C. 2950. 034 (former R.C. 2950.031) applies prospectively only).
