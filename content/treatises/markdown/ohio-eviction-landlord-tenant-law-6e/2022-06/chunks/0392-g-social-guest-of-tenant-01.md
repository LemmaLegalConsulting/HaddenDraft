## Source text

The Shroades/Sikora notice applies to the R.C. 5321.04 negligence per se claim of a tenant’s social guest. E.g., Patton v. Harper, No. 95-CA-20, 1996 WL 96007 (Ohio App., 4th Dist., Mar. 4, 1996). See also Primes v. Milbry , No. 18236, 1997 WL 760698, at *2 n.2 (Ohio App., 9th Dist., Nov. 19, 1997) (acknowledges the Patton holding, but does not address the issue because it resolves the case on other grounds).

For a discussion on the landlord’s R.C. 5321.04(A) obligations to a tenant’s social guests, see Section VII.C.1.b, above.
