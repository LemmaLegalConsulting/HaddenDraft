# 1. Party’s Right to a Trial by Jury

## Source text

Any party in an eviction action has a right to a trial by jury, provided the jury demand is filed timely, the requisite advance deposits are paid timely or waived, and the tenant has not waived the right to a trial by jury.

The right to a trial by jury in an eviction action is established in R.C. 1923.10.
