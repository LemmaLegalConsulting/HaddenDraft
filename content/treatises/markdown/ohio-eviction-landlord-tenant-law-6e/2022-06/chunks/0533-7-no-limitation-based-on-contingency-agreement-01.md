# 7. No Limitation Based on Contingency Agreement

## Table-of-contents context

## Source text

When a contingency agreement exists, the amount of the attorney fee award under R.C. Chapter 5321 is not limited to the amount specified in the contingency agreement.

Bynum v. Huffman, No. 57730, 1990 WL 210225 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Dec. 20, 1990).

The reasonable attorney’s fees that the court awards may exceed the contingency
amount. Id.
