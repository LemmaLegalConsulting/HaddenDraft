## Source text

Court decisions awarding general damages in an R.C. 5321.04 contract claim are set forth below, along with the R.C. 5321.04 violation, the contract rent , and the amount of the general damages awarded.

Bynum v. Cotterman , No. 26222, 2015 WL 751720 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Feb. 20, 2015) R.C. 5321.04 violation: bed bug infestation for 11 months; lack of use of bathtub (due to poor drainage) for two weeks. Contract rent: $800 per month. Damage award: $200 per month for 11 months (total of $2,200) for bed bug infestation; $200 for lack of use of bathtub for two weeks.

Liggett v. Shitaker Props., No. 23425, 2010 WL 1410758 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Apr.

9, 2010) R.C. 5321.04 violation: infestation of roaches and mice– tenant “killing thirty to forty roaches a day, two to three mice a day” – causing “conditions in the unit [that] were intolerable and inexcusable.” Contract rent: $525 per month. Damage award: $525 per month for two months, total of $1,050 (released to tenant from rent deposit).

Heck v. Whitehurst Co., No. L-03-1134, 2004 WL 1857131 (Ohio App., 6th Dist., Aug.

20, 2004) R.C. 5321.04 violation: cigarette smoke from neighbor’s apartment precluded use of 409 master bedroom and closet. Contract rent: $639 per month. Damage award: $319.50 per month for 12 months, total of $3,834.

Pleasant v. Greenfield Station Apts. , No. 152 82, 1995 WL 614359 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Oct. 20, 1995) R.C. 5321.04 violation: Failure to repair air conditioner, which caused unbearable temperatures in the apartment. Contract rent: $629 per month. Damage award: total of $629 for seven weeks.

Houshour v. Pfisterer, No. 2021-CVG-350 (Ohio Mun., Canton, June 10, 2021) R.C. 5321.04 violation: failure to provide adequate he at. Contract rent: $ 375 per
month. Damage award: $2,675 ($375 monthly rent amount for six months, plus $425 security deposit for another rental unit).

Johnson v. Rahim , No. 201 9-CVG-008127 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, Hous. Div., Jan.

21, 2020) R.C. 5321.04 violation: Lack of water service for 77 days. Contract rent: $ 927 per
month. Damage award: $2,310.

ATKM Mgmt. Group LLC v. Talley, No. 2018-CVG-018737 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland,
Hous. Div., Dec. 30, 2019) R.C. 5321.04 violation: Mold for seven months; vermin infestation and furnace not working properly for five months . Contract rent: $ 775 per month. Damage award (mold): $350 ($50 per month for seven months ); Damage award (vermin and faulty furnace): $250 ($50 per month for five months).

Baer v. Maynard, No. LLT-18-5 (Ohio Mun., Marion Cty., Apr. 29, 2019) R.C. 5321.04 violation: Frequent water and sewage backup in the basement, for five
months. Contract rent: $675 per month. Damage award: $1,750 ($350 per month, for five months).

Hamilton v. Dickerson, No. 2016-CVG-4233 (Ohio Mun., Franklin Cty., Jan. 2, 2018) R.C. 5321.04 violation: Lack of water service for 13 days. Contract rent: $450 per
month. Damage award: $200.

Brown v. Williams , No. 2012-CVG-17491 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, Hous. Div., Mar.

11, 2014, and May 2, 2014) R.C. 5321.04 violation: Lack of water service, the result of which tenant “lost almost all use of the property for the two weeks that the water was off.” Contract rent: $641.

Damage award: $320 (the full contract rent for the two-week period).

Ronny v. Bowman , No. 2007 -CVG-24072 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, Hous. Div., June 27, 2008, and Mar. 9, 2010) R.C. 5321.04 vio lation: “primarily cosmetic” – breaker box knockout caps, tub caulking, porch painting, motion detector bulb, yard debris, step handrails, carpet attachment on stairs, smoke detector, and floor support for weak areas. Contract rent:

$772 per month. Damage award: $50 per month for six months, total of $300.

H-1 Mgmt. LLC v. Perryman , No. 2006 -CVG-28200 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, Hous.

Div., July 10, 2007) R.C. 5321.04 violation: lack of heat for two weeks; water leak to basement from ducts;

kitchen ceiling water damage, hole and major cracks, evidencing water leak; bedroom outlets not grounded; missing or broken cover plate to wires hanging by furnace; and defective paint on exterior. Contract rent: $520 per month. Damage award (lack of heat for two weeks): $ 195 (75% reduction of contract rent). Damage award (other defective conditions): $50 per month for nine months, total of $450.

Rosier v. Newman, No. CVE-0500771 (Ohio Mun., Washington Court House, Mar. 5, 2007) R.C. 5321.04 violation: laundry room (roof l eak, light fixture inoperative, paint chipping, washer drain backflow); kitchen (ceiling leak, pipes to upstairs apartment leak, sink feedline leak, cabinets defective); bathroom (tub drain clog, hot water without pressure); front porch (deck and handraildefects); backyard (debris of shingles and nails); basement (waste-water clean-up and defective pipes). Contract rent: $425.

Damage award: $225 per month for 12 months, total of $2,700.

Francis v. Peters, No. 05-CVG-1116 (Ohio Mun., Chillicothe, Sept. 21, 2006) R.C. 5321.04 violation: basement flooding and electrical service problems, including the circuit breaker often kicking off, causing a loss of power to the house and, therefore, a loss of the furnace and hot water heater. Contr act rent: $425 per month. Damage award: $125 per month for 20 months, total of $2,500.

Clark v. Schiff, No. 05-CVG-1103 (Ohio Mun., Chillicothe, July 24, 2006) R.C. 5321.04 violation: defective kitchen window, roof leak, and defective carpet.

Contract rent: $375 per month. Damage award: $175 per month for 3.5 months, total of $615.

Dickson v. Lancaster, No. 06-CVG-288 (Ohio Mun., Jackson Cty., May 18, 2006) R.C. 5321.04 violation: roof leak, causing ceiling to fall in one room, making the room
unusable. Contract rent: $400 per month. Damage award: $266.67 (pro-rated value of one of six rooms ($66.66 per month) for four months).

411 Jackson v. George, No. 05-CVF-01761 (Ohio Mun., East Cleveland, Feb. 16, 2006) R.C. 5321.04 violation: lack of wate r service, due to landlord’s failure to pay water
bill. Contract rent: $811. Damage award: $300 per month for four months, total of $1,200.

Howard v. Reeves, No. 05-CVG-1910 (Ohio Mun., Chillicothe, Jan. 26, 2006) R.C. 5321.04 violation: electrical service problems; exposed electrical wires; defective duct work, doors, and windows; and construction debris. Contract rent: $500 per
month. Damage award: $164.26 per month for six months, total of $985.58 (released to tenant from rent deposit).

Crawley v. Brown, No. 2005-CVG-16627 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, Hous. Div., Oct. 6, 2005) R.C. 5321.04 violation: inadequate heat, exposed electrical wiring, water damage, loose/broken/missing flooring in kitchen, hole in chimney opening into a room, holes in bathroom ceilings, kitchen sink leak, missing lock hardware on rear entrance door.

Contract rent: $507. Damage award: $101 per month for seven months, total of $707.

Caraballo v. Moreno, No. 2005-CVG-00330 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, Hous. Div., Sept.

7, 2005) R.C. 5321.04 violation: kitchen sink drain problem, requiring tenant to cart water between bathroom and kitchen and to wash dishes in bucket; bedroom electrical outlet and overhead light not functional, requiring use of extension cord from kitchen to bedroom; balcony unsafe. Contract rent: $475 per month. Damage award: $200 per month for 6.5 months, total of $1,300.

Harris v. Winston , No. 2004 -CVG-28660 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, Hous. Div., Mar.

16, 2005) R.C. 5321.04 violation: Collapsed ceiling (preventing use ofproperty for four months);

water leak from upstairs toilet into the unit; kitchen sink back-up; windows not closing properly; insufficient heat due to furnace defects; and lead paint. Contract rent: $555 per month. Damage award (collapsed ceiling): $555 per month for four months, total of $2,220. Damage award (other defective conditions): $305 per month for 11 months plus five days, total of $3,405.
