# 2. Purpose and Scope of R.C. Chapter 5313

## Table-of-contents context

- XXIII. Land Installment Contracts,
  - A. Introduction
    - 2. Purpose and Scope of R.C. Chapter 5313

## Source text

“R.C. Chapter 5313 is essentially a ‘consumer protection law’ designed to ‘prevent a windfall to a vendor who has previously collected substantial sums under a land contract and/or has actually recovered the property.’” Voska v. Coffman , No. S -13- 008, 2013 WL 6579576, ¶ 9 (Ohio App., 6th Dist., Dec. 13, 2013) (quoting Howard v.

Temple, 172 Ohio App.3d 21, 872 N.E.2d 1260, ¶ 9 (4th Dist. 2007)).

R.C. Chapter 5313 regulates land installment contracts for the purchase of residential dwellings only. R.C. 5313.01(B). See Shepard’s Mobile Home Park, Ltd.

631
v. Sigmon, No. 14-CA-61, 2014 WL 4923972 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., Sept. 29, 2014);

P.M.D. Land Co. v. Warner Realty, Inc. , No. 2009-T-0021, 2009 WL 489554 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Dec. 18, 2009); Smith v. Jewett, No. 04-CA-96, 2005 WL 1840025 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., Aug. 2, 2005); Johnson v. Maxwell, 51 Ohio App.3d 137, 554 N.E.2d 1370 (9th Dist. 1988); DiYorio v. Porter, No. 81-CA-5, 1981 WL 4734 (Ohio App., 7th Dist., June 24, 1981).

R.C. Chapter 5313 applies irrespective of whether the residential dwelli ng is a single-family dwelling or a multifamily dwelling. Akron First Seventh Day Adventist Church v. Smith, No. 11577, 1984 WL 3956 (Ohio App., 9th Dist., Sept. 27, 1984).

R.C. Chapter 5313 applies to real property on which a house trailer or mobile home is parked at the time of contracting. Ohio Att’y Gen. Op. No. 73-042 (May 4, 1973).

But it does not apply to an agreement to purchase only a mobile home because the purchase fails to convey title to real property.Army v. Dunlap, 101 N.E.3d 1085, 2017- Ohio-9084, ¶ 29 (Ohio App., 3rd Dist., 2017); Shepard’s Mobile Home Park, Ltd. v.

Sigmon, No. 14-CA-61, 2014 WL 4923972, ¶ 12 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., Sept. 29, 2014).

R.C. Chapter 5313 does not apply to real property on which a recreational vehicle is parked at the time of contracting because the vehicle is not a dwelling. Simes v.

Beaver Valley Resorts, Inc., No. 2925, 1992 WL 274656 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Oct.

6, 1992).

R.C. Chapter 5313 does not apply to commercial real property. Retirement Mgmt.
Co. v. Nsong, 94 N.E.3d 1077, 2017-Ohio-5869, ¶ 17 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., 2017).
