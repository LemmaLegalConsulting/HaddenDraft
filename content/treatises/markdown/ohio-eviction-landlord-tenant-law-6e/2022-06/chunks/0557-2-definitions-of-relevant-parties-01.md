# 2. Definitions of Relevant Parties

## Source text

The relevant parties in Ohio’s landlord-tenant law for mhparks are defined in R.C.

4781.01. A “manufactured home park operator” or “park operator” is “the person who has responsible charge of a manufactured home park and who is licensed under sections 4781.26 to 4781.35 of the Revised Code.” R.C. 4781.01(M). A “tenant” is “a person who is entitled under a rental agreement with a manufactured home park operator to occupy a manufactured home park lot and who does not own the home occupying the lot.” R.C. 4781.01(T). An “owner” is “a person who is entitled under a rental agreement with a manufactured home park operator to occupy a manufactured home park lot and who owns the home occupying the lot.” R.C. 4781.01(U). A “resident” is “a person entitled under a rental agreement to the use and occupancy of residential premises to the exclusion of others.” R.C. 4781.01(V). Resident includes both tenants and owners. Id. For further discussion on these definitions, see Section IV.A., above.
