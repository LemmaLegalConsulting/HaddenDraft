## Source text

Since attorney’s fees under R.C. Chapter 53 21 are taxed as costs (see Section XIX.C, above), in a trial by jury, the award of attorney’s fees is a matter for the court, not the jury. Christe v. GMS Mgmt. Co., 88 Ohio St.3d 376, 726 N.E.2d 497 (2000);

Fant v. DiSabato , No. 87AP -265, 1987 WL 32190 ( Ohio App., 10th Dist., Dec. 29, 1987); Drake v. Menczer, 67 Ohio App.2d 122, 425 N.E.2d 961 (8th Dist. 1980).
