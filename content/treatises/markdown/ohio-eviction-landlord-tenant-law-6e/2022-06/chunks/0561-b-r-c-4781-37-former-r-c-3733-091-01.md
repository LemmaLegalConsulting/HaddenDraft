# b. R.C. 4781.37 (Former R.C. 3733.091)

## Source text

Fay Gardens Mobile Home Park v. Newman , 14 Ohio App.3d 144, 470 N.E.2d 164 (12th Dist. 1983) A resident who is in default in the payment of rent may not assert a retaliation defense under R.C. 3733.09 and may not recover damages for the park operator’s retaliation.

Voyager Vill., Ltd. v. Williams, 3 Ohio App.3d 288, 444 N.E.2d 1337 (2nd Dist. 1982) R.C. 3733.091(A)(3) does not apply when a park operator serves a month -to-month resident with a notice of termination because of the resident’s R.C. 3733.09(A) activities.
