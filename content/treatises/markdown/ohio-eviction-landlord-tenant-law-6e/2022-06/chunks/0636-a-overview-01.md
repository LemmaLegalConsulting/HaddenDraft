## Source text

The termination of a land installment contract under R.C. 5313.07 or 5313.08 is an exclusive remedy, which generally bars further action on the contract. R.C. 5313.10.

645 “Following foreclosure and judicial sale, R.C. 5313.07 allows a vendor to recover the proceeds from the judicial sale ‘up to and including the unpaid balance due on the land installment contract.’” Casto v. Prokop, No. 93-T-4853, 1995 WL 407217, at *2 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., June 9, 1995). Accord Castro v. Prokop, No. 89-T-4238, 1991 WL 35640 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Ma r. 15, 1991); Dalton v. Acker , 5 Ohio App.3d 150, 450 N.E.2d 288 (5th Dist. 1981).

The vendor may not recover a deficiency judgment against the vendee. 396 R.C.

5313.10. See Howard v. Temple , 172 Ohio App.3d 21, 872 N.E.2d 1260 (4th Dist.

2007); Castro v. Prokop, No. 89-T-4238, 1991 WL 35640 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Mar.

15, 1991); Good Shepherd Baptist Church v. City of Columbus, 20 Ohio App.3d 228, 485 N.E.2d 725 (10th Dist. 1984); Shone v. Griffis, No. 8252, 1984 WL 4376 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Feb. 2, 198 4); Dalton v. Acker, 5 Ohio App.3d 150, 450 N.E.2d 288 (5th Dist. 1981).

But the vendor may recover damages: (1) for the difference, if any, between the amount paid by the vendee on the contract and the fair rental value of the property, and
(2) for any deterioration or destruction of the property caused by the vendee. R.C.

5313.10. See Anna Holdings, LLC v. McClanahan, No. 148 N.E.3d 1255, 2019-Ohio- 4697 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., 2019); Voska v. Coffman , No. S -13-008, 2013 WL 6579576 (Ohio App., 6th Dist., Dec. 13, 2013); Howard v. Temple, 172 Ohio App.3d 21, 872 N.E.2d 1260 (4th Dist. 2007); Casto v. Prokop , No. 93 -T-4853, 1995 WL 407217 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., June 9, 1995); White v. Daniel, No. 12319, 1991 WL 262675 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Dec. 10,1991); Castro v. Prokop, No. 89-T-4238, 1991 WL 35640 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Mar. 15, 1991); Frey v. Hibbard, 62 Ohio App.3d 781, 577 N.E.2d 669 (1st Dist. 1989); Tanner v. Fulk, No. CA-2297, 1985 WL 6462 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., Aug. 1, 1985); Good Shepher d Baptist Church v. City of Columbus, 20 Ohio App.3d 228, 485 N.E.2d 725 (10th Dist. 1984); Butler v. Michel, 14 Ohio App.3d 116, 470 N.E.2d 217 (9th Dist. 1984).

For these purposes, the amount paid by the vendee includes the amount of the vendee’s initial deposit (if any). Doty v. Potteiger , No. 2017 -T-0029, 2017 WL 5998569 (Ohio App., 11th Dist., Dec. 4, 2017).
