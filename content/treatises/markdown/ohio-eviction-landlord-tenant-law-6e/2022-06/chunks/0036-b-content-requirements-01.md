## Source text

The only content requirement for an R.C. 5321.17 notice is that it must inform the tenant of the termination of the tenancy. The notice is not required to include any particular language.

However, it must “unequivocally inform” the tenant that the tenancy will be terminated as of a certain date. Gordon v. Morris, No. 2000-CA-69, 2001 WL 85797, at *5 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Feb. 2, 2001); Reith v. Skruck, 71 Ohio Misc.2d 1, 2, 653 N.E.2d 755 (Ohio Mun., Wadsworth, 1995).
