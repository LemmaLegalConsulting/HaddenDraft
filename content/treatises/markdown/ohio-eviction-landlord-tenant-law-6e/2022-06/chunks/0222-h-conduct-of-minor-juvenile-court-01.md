## Source text

The activity of a minor, for which a juvenile court has jurisdiction, may constitute criminal activity for purposes of an assisted housing lease and HUD regulations.

Cincinnati Metro. Hous. Auth. v. Browning , No. C -010055, 2002 WL 63491 (Ohio App., 1st Dist., Jan. 18, 2002). See also CMJ Mgmt. Co. v. Wilkerson, 91 Mass. App.
Ct. 276, 75 N.E.3d 605 (2017); Hous. Auth. for Prince George’s County v. Williams, 784 A.2d 621 (Md. App. 2001).
