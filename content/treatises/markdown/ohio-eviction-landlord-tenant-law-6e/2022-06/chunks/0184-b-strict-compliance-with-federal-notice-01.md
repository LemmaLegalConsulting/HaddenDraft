Ross, No. C-120045, 2012 WL 6674412 (Ohio App., 1st Dist., Dec. 21, 2012) (project- based subsidized housing). “As the operator of federally subsidized housing, Hidden Meadows had to comply with federal regulations governing the program, HUD’s multi-family occupancy handbook, and HUD’s model lease.”Id., ¶ 37.

In addition, when an assisted housing lease includes (explicitly or by r eference) the notice requirements of an inapplicable federal regulation, the landlord must comply with the notice requirements of the applicable federal regulations, as well as the inapplicable regulations that the lease incorporated. Sandefur Co. v. Jone s, 9 Ohio App.3d 85, 87, 458 N.E.2d 390 (10th Dist. 1982).
