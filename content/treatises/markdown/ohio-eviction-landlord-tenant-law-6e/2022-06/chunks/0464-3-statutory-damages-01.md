If any portion of the security deposit is wrongfully withheld, the landlord’s liability for statutory damages under R.C. 5321.16(C) is mandatory. Smith v. Padgett, 32 Ohio St.3d 344, 513 N.E.2d 737 (1987).335 A tenant’s failure to demand the statutory damages in the pleadings does not waive the tenant’s right to recover them. Ohio R. Civ. P. 54(C). 336 E.g., Leszczynski v.

Brewer, No. 12523, 1991 WL 285435 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Dec. 27, 1991). Cf.

Morales v. Windsor Realty & Mgmt., Inc. , No. 2000 -CVH-002037 (Ohio Mun., Cleveland, Hous. Div., Nov. 21, 2000) (R.C. 5321.16(C) attorney’s fees).
