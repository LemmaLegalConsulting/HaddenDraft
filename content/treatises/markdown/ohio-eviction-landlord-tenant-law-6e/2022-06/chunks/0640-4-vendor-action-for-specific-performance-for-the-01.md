# 4. Vendor Action for Specific Performance for the

## Source text

Purchase Price When a vendee breaches a land installment contract, the vendor may elect “to exercise the option of accelerating the payments under the Land Contract and bringing a suit against [the vendee] to compel the performance of the contract.” Arnett v.

Bardonaro, No. 25371, 2013 WL 1189009, ¶ 37 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Mar. 22, 2013).

In the action for specific performance, the vendor is electing to receive the purcha se price in exchange for the property. Id.

The action for specific performance is an alternative option to the vendor’s right to bring an action for possession under R.C. 5313.07,.08. Id., ¶ 35.
