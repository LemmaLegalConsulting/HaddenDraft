# e. R.C. 5321.07 Deposits Constitute Rent Paid

## Source text

Any “rent money deposited with the clerk of court pursuant to R.C. 5321.07 constitutes rent.” Davis v. Wesolowski, 146 N.E.3d 633, 2020 -Ohio-677, ¶ 15 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., 2020). Therefore, a landlord may not consider these deposits unpaid rent for R.C. 5321.16 purposes. Id., ¶¶ 17-20.
