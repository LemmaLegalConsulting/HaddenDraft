“Simply because the attorney fees incurred in this case were greatly disproportionate to the damages recovered on [tenant’s] security deposit claim does not mean they were unreasonable. As stated above, R.C. 5321.16 is a remedial statute intended to en sure that a tenant incurs no expense when seeking a refund of a wrongfully withheld security deposit. Consistent with th at purpose, [the tenant] was entitled to recover reasonable attorney fees [the landlord’s] actions forced her to incur to enforce her statutory rights.” Alcorso v. Correll, No. 110218, 2021 WL 4317396, ¶ 49 (Ohio App., 8th Dist., Sept. 23, 2021) ($26,825 fee award, $1,200 judgment).
