Security Deposit When a tenant has violated an obligation to pay a security deposit, at the end of the tenancy, the landlord may not recover both the unpaid security deposit and the full amount of any property damage (or unpaid rent) claim. Wayne Mgmt. Co. v. McGill,
No. 81-CA-57, 1982 WL 3741 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., June 2, 1982); Hall v. Leonard,
No. 94-CG-667 (Ohio Mun., Marietta, Mar. 7, 1995).
