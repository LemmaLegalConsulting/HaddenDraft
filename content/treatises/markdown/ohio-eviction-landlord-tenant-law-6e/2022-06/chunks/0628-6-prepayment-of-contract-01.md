# 6. Prepayment of Contract

## Table-of-contents context

- XXIII. Land Installment Contracts,
  - B. Contract Requirements and Related

## Source text

A vendee does not hav e a right to prepay the outstanding principal and accrued interest on a land installment contract, unless the contract so provides. DiMarco v.

Shay, 154 Ohio App.3d 141, 796 N.E.2d 572 (10th Dist. 2003).E.g., Lowry v. Buroker,
No. 2017 -CA-25, 2017 WL 66 28496 (Ohio App., 2nd Dist., Dec. 29, 2017) (land installment contract expressly provided for prepayment, and based on that provision the court granted the vendee specific performance).
