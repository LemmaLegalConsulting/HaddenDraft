# 4. Damage Claim Less Than the Security Deposit

## Table-of-contents context

- XVIII. Landlord Claim for Property
  - E. Defense Issues
    - 4. Damage Claim Less Than the Security Deposit

## Source text

When the amount of the landlord’s R.C. 5321.05(C) damage claim is less than the amount of the tenant’s security deposit, and the tenant has forfeited the security deposit, the landlord has not suffered any “actual damages” as a result of the tenant’s R.C. 5321.05(A) violation. Hines v. Riley , 129 Ohio App.3d 379, 717 N.E.2d 1133 (4th Dist. 1998).
