# c. Improper Rent Deposit

## Source text

Nonpayment of rent is not good cause to terminate an assisted housing tenancy if the tenant timely deposited rent with the clerk of court under R.C. 5321.07 and, thereafter, the court released the deposited rent to the landlord because of the tenant’s noncompliance with the R.C. 5321.07 written notice requirement. Real Props. Servs.
Mgmt. v. Anatra, No. 3-96-22, 1997 WL 430778 (Ohio App., 3rd Dist., July 30, 1997);
