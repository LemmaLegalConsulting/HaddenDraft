# 5. Probate Court

## Source text

With respect to real property in a probate proceeding, R.C. 2101.24 does not provide the court of common pleas, probate division, with exclusive jurisdiction over an executor’s claim for possession against an occupant of the property. Estate of Wos
v. Wos, No. L-05-1408, 2006 WL 2383966 (Ohio App., 6th Dist., Aug. 18, 2006).

The executor may bring an eviction action against the occupant under R.C. Chapter
1923. Id. If the occupant is not a tenant, the executor may bring the eviction action under R.C. 192 3.02(A)(5), which permits eviction actions against occupiers of land who are without color of title. Id.
