# e. Discount Rent or Late Payment Fee

## Source text

A late payment fee issue arises when a lease purports to establish both a rental amount and a so-called discount amount for payment by a particular date. The issue is whether the discount amount is actually the base rent and, therefore, the difference between the discount amount and the rental amount is a late fee. For a discussion on this issue, see Friedman v. Krupp Corp., 282 Ill. App.3d 436, 668 N.E.2d 142 (1996).
