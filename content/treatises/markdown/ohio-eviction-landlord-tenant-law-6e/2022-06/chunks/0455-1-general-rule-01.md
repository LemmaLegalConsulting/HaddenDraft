## Source text

At the end of the tenancy, the landlord may apply the security deposit “to the payment of any past due rent and to the payment of the amount of damages that the landlord has suffered by reason of the tenant’s noncompliance with Section 5321.05 of the Revised Code or the rental agreement.”328 R.C. 5321.16(B).

“[W]ithin thirty days after termination of the rental agreement and delivery of possession,” the landlord is required to return the security deposit to the tenant and/or provide the tenant with a written itemization of any portion of the security deposit that 328 For a discussion on landlord claims for past due rent, see Chapter XVII, below. For a discussion on landlord claims under R.C. 5321.05(C) for property damage, see Chapter XVIII, below.

the landlord retains. Id.

The tenant is required to provide the landlord with a forwarding address to which the security deposit and/or written itemization may be sent. Id.
