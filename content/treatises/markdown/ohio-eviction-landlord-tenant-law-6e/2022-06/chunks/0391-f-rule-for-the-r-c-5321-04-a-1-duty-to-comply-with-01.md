Applicable Codes The Shroades/Sikora notice applies to R.C. 5321.04(A)(1) 310 cases. Sikora v.

Wenzel, 88 Ohio St.3d 493, 495, 727 N.E.2d 1277 (2000). E.g., Stancil v. K.S.B. Inv.

& Mgmt. Co., 62 Ohio App.3d 765, 577 N.E.2d 452 (8th Dist. 1991).

In R.C. 5321.04(A)(1) cases, the notice is not required to state that the defective condition is a code violation. Martin v. Edgewater Props., No. 92-CA-005307, 1992 WL 357321 (Ohio App., 9th Dist., Nov. 25, 1992).
