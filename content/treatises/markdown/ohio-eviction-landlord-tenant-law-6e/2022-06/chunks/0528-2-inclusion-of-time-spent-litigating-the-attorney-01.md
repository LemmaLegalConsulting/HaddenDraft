## Source text

Fee Award In an R.C. Chapter 5321 claim for attorney’s fees, the moving party may recover attorney’s fees for the time spent litigating the fee request, as well as for time spent litigating and enforcing the underlying judgment. Hall v. Lacheta, No. 92AP-020013, 1992 WL 362546 (Ohio App., 5th Dist., Nov. 18, 1992). Cf. Westbourne Dev. Co. v.

Sigma Sys. Health Mgmt. Specialists, Inc. , No. 91AP -856, 1992 WL 11197 (Ohio App., 10th Dist., Jan. 16, 1992) (att orney’s fees under commercial lease). But see Alexander v. LJF Mgmt. Inc., No. C-100618, 2011 WL 2112623 (Ohio App., 1st Dist., May 27, 2011) (R.C. 5321.16(C)).
