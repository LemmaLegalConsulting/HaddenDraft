## Source text

R.C. 1923.06 establishes the time period by which service of process in an eviction action must be completed. This requirement is discussed in Section I.G.2, below.

R.C. 1923.06 purports to establish service of process rules for complaints that allege either an eviction claim only or both eviction and non-eviction claims.28 Some of these R.C. 1923.06 rules conflict with Civil Rules 4.1 and 4.6. These rules and conflicts are discussed in Section I.G.3, below.

R.C. 1923.051 establishes service of process rules for drug -related R.C.

1923.02(A)(6) eviction actions. These rules are discussed in Section I.G.4, below.
