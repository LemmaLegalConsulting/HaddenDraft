## Source text

R.C. Chapter 5321 applies to residential tenancies. R.C. 5321.01. See also Mann
v. Northgate Investors, L.L.C. , 138 Ohio St.3d 175, 5 N.E.3d 594, ¶ 8 (2014);

Vardeman v. Llewellyn, 17 Ohio St.3d 24, 26, 476 N.E.2d 1038 (1985); Shroades v.

Rental Homes, Inc., 68 Ohio St.2d 20, 21-22, 427 N.E.2d 774 (1981).

It does not apply to commercial tenancies. Maggiore v. Kovach, 101 Ohio St.3d 184, 187-88, 803 N.E.2d 790 (2004); Hendrix v. Eighth & Walnut Corp., 1 Ohio St.3d 205, 208, 438 N.E.2d 1149 (1982).

It establishes rights and responsibilities of landlords and tenants who are parties to a rental agreement for residential premises. R.C. 5321.01.See also Mann v. Northgate Investors, L.L.C. , 138 Ohio St.3d 175, 5 N.E.3d 594, ¶ 8 (2014); Vardeman v.

Llewellyn, 17 Ohio St.3d 24, 26, 476 N.E.2d 1038 (1985); Shroades v. Rental Homes, Inc., 68 Ohio St.2d 20, 21-22, 427 N.E.2d 774 (1981).

The definition and scope of the terms rental agreement, tenant, landlord, and residential premises are discussed, in turn, in Sections VI.B-F, below.
