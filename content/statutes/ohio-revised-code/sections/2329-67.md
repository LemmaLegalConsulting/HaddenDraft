# Ohio Rev. Code § 2329.67 — Exemption not subject to payment of cost of proceedings to collect debt.

The exempt personal earnings provided for in division (A)(13) of section

2329.66

of the Revised Code are not subject to the payment of the cost of any proceedings brought to recover a judgment for a debt or to satisfy a judgment for a debt.
