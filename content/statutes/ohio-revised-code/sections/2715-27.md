# Ohio Rev. Code § 2715.27 — Claim for causing death or injury.

When the plaintiff's claim is for causing death or personal injury by a negligent or wrongful act, the bond required by section

2715.26

of the Revised Code shall be in such amount as is fixed by the court where the action is pending, or a judge thereof if application is made in vacation.
