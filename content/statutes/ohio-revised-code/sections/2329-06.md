# Ohio Rev. Code § 2329.06 — Lien of a judgment when action removed to supreme court.

When a certificate is filed as provided in section

2329.02

of the Revised Code, the lien of a judgment of the court of common pleas in an action which is appealed by the judgment debtor to the court of appeals and thence removed into the supreme court shall not thereby be divested or vacated, but shall be continued until the final determination of the action in the supreme court.
