# Ohio Rev. Code § 2329.151 — Conduct of judicial sale.

Except as provided in sections

2329.152

to

2329.154

of the Revised Code, all public auctions of goods, chattels, or lands levied upon by execution shall be conducted personally by one of the following:

(A) An officer of the court;

(B) For the public auction of goods and chattels, a resident of this state licensed as an auctioneer under Chapter 4707. of the Revised Code;

(C) For the public auction of lands, a private selling officer.
