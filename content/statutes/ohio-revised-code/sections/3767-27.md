# Ohio Rev. Code § 3767.27 — Inspector of nuisances.

The board of county commissioners, whenever there is a violation of sections

3767.13

to

3767.29

, inclusive, of the Revised Code, may employ and reasonably compensate one inspector of nuisances who shall be vested with police powers and authorized to examine all cases of violation of such sections.
