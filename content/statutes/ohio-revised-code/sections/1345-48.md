# Ohio Rev. Code § 1345.48 — Deceptive acts or practices - damages.

(A) Failure to comply with sections

1345.41

to

1345.50

of the Revised Code constitutes a deceptive act or practice in connection with a consumer transaction in violation of section

1345.02

of the Revised Code.

(B) If the seller of a prepaid entertainment contract fails to comply with division (D) (4) (a) of section

1345.44

of the Revised Code, the buyer may recover the amount of money due to him under that section and, in addition, may recover damages in an amount equal to the amount of money due to him and reasonable attorney's fees.
