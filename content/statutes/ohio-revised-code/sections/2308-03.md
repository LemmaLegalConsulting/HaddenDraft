# Ohio Rev. Code § 2308.03 — Securing and protecting vacant and abandoned property.

(A) I f a residential property is found to be vacant and abandoned under section

2308.02

of the Revised Code, a mortgagee on the residential property may enter that property to secure and protect it from damage.

(B) A mortgagee that has not filed a residential mortgage loan foreclosure action on a property for which the mortgagee holds a mortgage may enter and secure that property only if the mortgage contract or other documents provide for such an entry.

(C) The equitable and statutory rights to redemption of a mortgage on a property found to be vacant and abandoned pursuant to section

2308.02

of the Revised Code expire upon the confirmation of sale of the property.
