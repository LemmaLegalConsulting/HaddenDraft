# Ohio Rev. Code § 2737.20 — Injunctive relief.

In addition to, or in lieu of, any other relief available under sections

2737.01

to

2737.19

of the Revised Code, the court may grant a temporary restraining order, preliminary injunction, or permanent injunction in accordance with the Rules of Civil Procedure.
