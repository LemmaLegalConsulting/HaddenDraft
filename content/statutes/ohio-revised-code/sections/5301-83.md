# Ohio Rev. Code § 5301.83 — Copies of environmental covenant to be provided.

(A) A copy of an environmental covenant shall be provided to all of the following in a manner required by the applicable agency:

(1) Each person that signed the environmental covenant;

(2) Each person holding a recorded interest in the real property that is subject to the environmental covenant;

(3) Each person in possession of the real property that is subject to the environmental covenant;

(4) Each unit of local government in which the real property that is subject to the environmental covenant is located;

(5) Any other person that the agency requires.

(B) The validity of an environmental covenant is not affected by failure to provide a copy of the environmental covenant as required under this section.
