# Ohio Rev. Code § 2329.025 — Fees.

Any person who files a foreign judgment pursuant to section

2329.022

of the Revised Code shall pay a filing fee of fifteen dollars. Fees for docketing, transcription, or other enforcement proceedings shall be as provided in section

2303.20

of the Revised Code.
