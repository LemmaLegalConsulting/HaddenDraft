# Ohio Rev. Code § 5303.23 — Order for sale and effect.

Upon the hearing, if it is proved that a sale of an estate described in section

5303.21

of the Revised Code would be for the benefit of the tenant in tail, or for life, and do no substantial injury to the heirs in tail, or others in expectancy, succession, reversion, or remainder, the court shall direct a sale to be made, the manner thereof and appoint some suitable person to make it. Such sale shall vest the estate sold in the purchaser, free from the entailment, limitation, or condition.
