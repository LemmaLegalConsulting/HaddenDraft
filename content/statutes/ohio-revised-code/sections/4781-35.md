# Ohio Rev. Code § 4781.35 — Prosecuting violations.

(A) No person shall violate sections

4781.26

to

4781.35

of the Revised Code or the rules adopted thereunder.

(B) The prosecuting attorney of the county, the city director of law, or the attorney general, upon complaint of the division of industrial compliance, shall prosecute to termination or bring an action for injunction against any person violating sections

4781.26

to

4781.35

of the Revised Code or the rules adopted thereunder.
