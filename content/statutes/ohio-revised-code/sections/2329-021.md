# Ohio Rev. Code § 2329.021 — Foreign judgment defined.

As used in sections

2329.021

to

2329.027

of the Revised Code, "foreign judgment" means any judgment, decree, or order of a court of the United States, or of any court of another state, that is entitled to full faith and credit in this state.
