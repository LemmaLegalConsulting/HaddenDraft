# Ohio Rev. Code § 1345.47 — Defenses of buyer against holder in due course.

## Source text

Notwithstanding section

1303.35

of the Revised Code, a buyer who executes a prepaid entertainment contract or a note in connection with such a contract may assert as a defense to a claim by a holder in due course, as defined in section

1303.32

of the Revised Code, any defense that the buyer may assert against the seller of the prepaid entertainment contract.
