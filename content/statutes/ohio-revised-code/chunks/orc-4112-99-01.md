# Ohio Rev. Code § 4112.99 — Civil penalty.

## Source text

(A) Whoever violates this chapter is subject to a civil action for damages, injunctive relief, or any other appropriate relief. Except as otherwise provided in division (B) of this section, a person may bring such a civil action in a court of competent jurisdiction.

(B) A person is prohibited from bringing a civil action for employment discrimination under this section.
