# Ohio Rev. Code § 1349.36 — Administrative rules.

## Source text

The superintendent of financial institutions may, in accordance with Chapter 119. of the Revised Code, adopt reasonable rules to administer and enforce sections

1349.25

to

1349.35

of the Revised Code and to carry out the purpose of those sections as stated in section

1349.32

of the Revised Code.
