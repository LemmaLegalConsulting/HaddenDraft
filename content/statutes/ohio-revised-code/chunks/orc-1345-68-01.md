# Ohio Rev. Code § 1345.68 — Effect on other obligations, rights, or remedies.

## Source text

Sections

1345.61

to

1345.68

of the Revised Code do not annul or limit any obligations, rights, or remedies that might otherwise be applicable or available under the laws of this state.
