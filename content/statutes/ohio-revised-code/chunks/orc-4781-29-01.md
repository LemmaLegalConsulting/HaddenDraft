# Ohio Rev. Code § 4781.29 — License - refusal to grant, suspension, revocation.

## Source text

The division of industrial compliance may refuse to grant, may suspend, or may revoke any license granted to any person for failure to comply with sections

4781.26

to

4781.35

of the Revised Code or with any rule adopted under section

4781.26

of the Revised Code.
