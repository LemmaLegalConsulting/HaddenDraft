# Ohio Rev. Code § 3767.26 — Judgment for fine and costs.

## Source text

A judgment for fine and costs rendered against a person or corporation for the violation of sections

3767.13

to

3767.29

, inclusive, of the Revised Code, when the defendant has no property or has not a sufficient amount within the county upon which to levy to satisfy such judgment and costs, may be enforced and collected in the manner in which judgments are collected in civil cases.
