# Ohio Rev. Code § 2329.026 — Optional procedure.

## Source text

Sections

2329.021

to

2329.027

of the Revised Code do not affect the right of a judgment creditor to bring an action to enforce a foreign judgment rather than to enforce the judgment pursuant to those sections.
