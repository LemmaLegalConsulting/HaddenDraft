# Ohio Rev. Code § 5301.18 — Deeds from state must recite facts.

## Source text

All deeds executed under sections

5301.15

,

5301.16

, and

5301.17

of the Revised Code must recite the facts, as ascertained by the governor and attorney general, upon the proof of which they are executed, and shall be recorded in the office of the director of administrative services.
