# Ohio Rev. Code § 4112.07 — Posting of notice.

## Source text

Every person subject to division (A), (B), (C), (D), or (E) of section

4112.02

of the Revised Code shall post in a conspicuous place or places on the person's premises, or on the internet in a manner that is accessible to the public, a notice to be prepared or approved by the commission that shall set forth excerpts of this chapter and other relevant information that the commission deems necessary to explain this chapter.
