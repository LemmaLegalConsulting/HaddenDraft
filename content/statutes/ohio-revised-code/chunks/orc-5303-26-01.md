# Ohio Rev. Code § 5303.26 — Proceeds of sale.

## Source text

All money arising from a sale of an estate described in section

5303.21

of the Revised Code, for purposes of descent, succession, reversion, or remainder, has the same character and is governed by the same principles as the estate sold, and passes according to the terms of the deed, will, or other instrument creating the estate.
