# Ohio Rev. Code § 5303.20 — Judgment and deed to convey land.

## Source text

If the court finds the allegations of the petition provided for in section

5303.18

of the Revised Code to be true, it may make an order authorizing the survivors to complete the contract by conveying the land. The deed shall recite the order, and shall convey as complete and perfect a title, and have the same effect, as if executed by all the owners.
