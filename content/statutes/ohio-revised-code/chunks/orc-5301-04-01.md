# Ohio Rev. Code § 5301.04 — Deed, mortgage, or lease of a married person.

## Source text

A deed, mortgage, or lease of any interest of a married person in real property shall be signed, acknowledged, and certified as provided in section

5301.01

of the Revised Code.
