# Ohio Rev. Code § 3767.07 — Court shall punish offender for violation of injunction or order.

## Source text

In case of the violation of any injunction or closing order, granted under sections

3767.01

to

3767.11

, inclusive, of the Revised Code, or of a restraining order or the commission of any contempt of court in proceedings under such sections, the court or, in vacation, a judge thereof, may summarily try and punish the offender. The trial may be had upon affidavits or either party may demand the production and oral examination of the witnesses.
