# Ohio Rev. Code § 2329.22 — Rule as to sale of lands by state.

## Source text

Sections

2329.19

to

2329.21

, inclusive, of the Revised Code do not affect the sale of lands by the state. All lands, the property of individuals, indebted to the state for debt, taxes, or in any other manner shall be sold without valuation for the discharge of such debt or taxes.
