# Ohio Rev. Code § 1309.624 — Waiver - UCC 9-624.

## Source text

(A) A debtor or secondary obligor may waive the right to notification of disposition of collateral under section

1309.611

of the Revised Code only by an agreement to that effect entered into and authenticated after default.

(B) A debtor may waive the right to require disposition of collateral under division (E) of section

1309.620

of the Revised Code only by an agreement to that effect entered into and authenticated after default.

(C) Except in a consumer-goods transaction, a debtor or secondary obligor may waive the right to redeem collateral under section

1309.623

of the Revised Code only by an agreement to that effect entered into and authenticated after default.
